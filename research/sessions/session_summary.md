**Dates:** Aug–Nov 2023

Six PCs independently arrive in Lowmarsh, a democratically governed city near the Mercia/Gribsby border. They are assembled via a prisoner deal: freedom in exchange for capturing a live ghoul to help brew a cure for Ghoul Fever spreading from the Greenwoods.

Key events:
- Nik Jones receives a vision from **Davy Jones** threatening to collect on his soul (his father, Captain Hook, used it as collateral)
- The **Three-Faced Lady** appears to Sibylla in feline form; tasks her with stopping a ritual to the northeast
- The party kills ghouls; Bigby is bitten and infected; Mordred and Drizella brew the cure at Sage Hospice
- The **Deer-Masked Stranger** (later revealed as Nimue) is seen surgically removing organs from dead Camlin soldiers
- The **Green Man** says one word to Mordred: *"Sunderance"*
- The **Red Hand** attacks Lowmarsh; the **Crooked Men** are active in town
- Thori Black (missing Treasurer) is found dead from psychic trauma; a forged confession framing him for theft is found alongside a strange cocoon
- Drizella receives warnings from the **Shadic Brethren** (Shadow Monks of the Faceless Soldier) to flee before they assassinate Lewis Short

The party boards the **Winter Express** (magical train through the Greenwoods) and arrives in **Rymes**, a major city in the Gribsby region. Rymes is home to Ersatz University and, unknown to all, the Tomb of the Horned King.

Key events:
- Party discovers the **Tomb of the Horned King** lies beneath Rymes; guarded by the Bonekeeper's Guild
- **Nimue** (unmasked from the Deer-Masked Stranger) attacks the party at the Ersatz University Observatory; she is in undead form, mentally controls Mordred, and takes Drizella's mask before fleeing
- Sibylla is revealed to be Nimue's second-to-last student (before Morgana)
- **Hazel the Wise** (grandmother of the Thrice-Triple Covens) questions the party; they learn Nimue bears Caliburn's scabbard
- **Dean Marikosa** reveals the Fall of Arcadia: the Horned King resisted Titania, Mab, Merlin, and nine Grandmother Hags before being slain; the Order of the Eleventh Eye *"placed their accursed mirror beneath the King"*
- **Savvan** (a changeling caught in Nimue's ritual) warns that a Sunderance will occur within 12–18 months
- **Memphis Cadwallader** (the Imp) enters the party's orbit
- On a caravan to Salasarglumm: **The Creature** (Frankenstein's monster) is travelling to find Victor Frankenstein; Lady Tremaine hired him to kill Anastasia
- Bigby's **brother** (the Big Bad Wolf) attempts to assassinate him on the caravan

## Arc 6: Pebblecreek (Sessions 34–43)
**Dates:** Apr–Jul 2025

The party arrives in **Pebblecreek** (a major city with the Sun Spire). Bigby and Mordred win a tournament. The antiseed conspiracy comes to a head.

Key events:
- **Bloody Mary** reveals her history: Merlin's student, Empress of the Arrnon Empire, imprisoned at age 22; claims the Order of the Eleventh Eye trapped her to stop her from reforming the Empire
- **Lancelot** (aged, white-haired ex-Knight of Camelot) is living in Pebblecreek; he left Camelot after Arthur said *"save the child"* as Guinevere died in childbirth
- **Krennic Silverbarrow** calls Drizella "Lady Tremaine" — implying news of Lady Tremaine's death has spread
- **Snow White** found in a greenhouse, afflicted with vampirism; she is **Queen Maeve's daughter**
- **Darian Sand** dies in battle at the docks (Session 41); **Mordred** uses **Caliburn** to stab a chest containing an antiseed — a silver dragon of light shoots skyward; the Horned King's eyes stare back from the Sun Spire
- **Briar Rose (Aurora)** is found sleeping in a cocoon of thorny briars in the Sun Spire greenhouse (Session 42); the party wakes her
- **Flycatcher** reveals: at least five antiseeds distributed; **Eamus Hogson** financed them; the mysterious **"M"** controls police and docks across Arrnonland
- Party departs Pebblecreek aboard the *Proud Mary* (Session 43)
- Mordred realises his sword "Clarent" may actually be **Caledfwlch** (the original Excalibur) due to anti-scrying runes; only he can lift it
- Queen **Titania** confirms Merlin's last words were *"Forgive me, Sister"*

## Arc 7: Rymes Return / Greenwoods / Grimordia (Sessions 44–52)
**Dates:** Aug–Oct 2025

Return to Rymes and Ersatz University; then into the Darkwoods and Grimordia (ruined capital of the Arrnon Empire).

Key events:
- **Henry Borsch** (necromancer student) kills Briar's familiar Bud; Bigby challenges him to a duel and wins
- **Nimue** reappears at the Observatory; **Mordred stabs Ida Montague's heart** — the antiseed dissolves into a **Tear of the Horned King** (a memory of him as the god of harvest and plenty)
- Drizella trades the **Ring of Peerless Apprehension** to Bloody Mary for passage through the Darkwoods (Session 47)
- **Drizella replaces her eyeball with a mirror shard** — Mary can now see through her eye; goblins steal the eye to the Goblin King
- **Grimordia** (Session 48): once a *"shining beacon of tolerance and power"*; now ruins with people frozen in stone, a black dragon circling; the party finds Merlin's four items in the vault
- Party recovers Merlin's items (Dragon Egg, Bracers of the Elements, Ring of Peerless Apprehension, Spectral Key) and escapes Grimordia

## Arc 8: Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire (Sessions 54–66)
**Dates:** Nov 2025 – Mar 2026

The party arrives in **Everdrift** (King Louie's Summer Court city in the great tree), attends the **Tourney of the Four Seasons**, and is drawn into Oz before landing in Cindershire.

Key events:
- **Equinox** (construct made by Maeve in Mordred's likeness) steals Mordred's sword; **King Oberon** reveals he was murdered 30 April 348 and suspects the dagger was Bigby's
- **Carabosse** (the Fairy Godmother) confesses to Briar: she worked with Bloody Mary and Lady Tremaine to engineer Drizella's entire life — *Drizella was always meant to free Mary from the mirror*
- **Bloody Mary freed** (Session 57): Party enters the Mirror of Aretas; Mordred uses Clarent/Caledfwlch as a key; Mary's hand (Hand of Sycorax) is restored; she grants boons and is freed; a dark deer with white eyes briefly appears
- **Mordred summons Morgana** at Everdrift (Session 59) — **three figures appear**: Morgana Le Fay, Queen Maeve, and Lady Tremaine — revealed as fragments of the original Morgana Pendragon
- **Medraut** (raven knight, Maeve's son, Mordred's mirror-image) reveals himself; says *"What she did to **us**"*
- **Nimue** (under compulsion) gives Mordred **Caliburn**; briefly says *"only the waters of Pendragon can free me"*
- **Robert Tremaine** (Drizella's father) rescued from Maeve's mirror prison; says to escape through mirrors
- **Robson Wellgood** tells Bigby to find him in Mercia; appears to be defecting from Silverheart Court
- **In Oz** (Sessions 60–61): party is *partly dead*; guided by **Glinda** (psychopomp); at the Three-Faced Lady's chapel they find beheaded bodies of young, middle-aged, and elderly women — **Morgana killed the Three-Faced Lady and split into three**
- **Glinda reveals each character's death**: Briar (in arms of her beloved), Drizella (content at her desk), Bigby (at hands of bitter rival), **Mordred (very soon, at the hands of his father)**
- **Wizard of Oz** (Barnabas Ragdoll) reveals the **Ruby Slippers** are now the **Glass Slippers** of Cindershire; one slipper can undo the Wish Ring that destroyed the druids
- **Briar agrees to the Fool's deal** (Session 66): returns from death; must sleep in 5 dragon lairs; vomits blacksmith's tongs
- **Anastasia Tremaine's memories restored** when Captain Sharmaine is killed (Session 66) — he held her memories in an object
- **Lady Tremaine confirmed alive** by the Fool / Polko — *"a club over the head would kill her"*

# Session 25 Summary

**Date:** ~Dec 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 25](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raCvBubSybQ)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

Session 25 picks up after the basilisk encounter. Bigby recovers from "stone sickness" — a rare affliction where the brain takes a day to reconnect after petrification. The party meets **Kristoff Marlo**, a fugitive wizard hiding in the sewers with the pickled basilisk corpse. Marlo provides a **Potion of Stone Passage** (pass through stone for 1 hour) and explains the **Seven Particles Theory** of consciousness.

The Beast King has used the **Ring of Objectification** to transform Darian into a lamp after Darian revealed his genie heritage at dinner. Darian's split consciousness (genie nature) causes him to remain aware across both the lamp and his parrot familiar — an abnormal side effect.

The party encounters **Cinderella** (actually Anastasia under modify memory, appearing as a shorter young woman in a bright blue dress with blonde hair) outside the castle. She warns of hidden threats and references the ball where Drizella was aged 20 years: "what happened at the ball was horrible." **Lady Tremaine** arrives in a massive palanquin carried by eight people, with her crow swarm (Nightshade). She claims she can reverse what happened to Drizella at the ball.

In a secondary plotline, the party witnesses a public execution and Bigby solves the case using forensic pollen analysis (critical success perception check of 30). The magistrate is revealed as **Governor Eustus Moro**, who secretly uses wax dummy heads to show mercy while maintaining public order. Moro offers an alliance to overthrow the Beast King: "Three Barons — we need two of them either on side or dead."

## Key Events

- Bigby recovers from stone sickness; meets Kristoff Marlo in the sewers
- Beast King transformed Darian into a lamp using the Ring of Objectification; Darian's consciousness split between lamp and parrot
- Party encounters Cinderella/Anastasia outside the castle (modified memory, different appearance)
- Lady Tremaine arrives in palanquin; claims she can reverse Drizella's aging from the ball

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Kristoff Marlo** — Fugitive wizard hiding in Salasarglumm's sewers. Brewed health potion for Bigby. Pickled the basilisk for research. Fluent in noble language (Phen). Explains the Seven Particles Theory of consciousness.
- **Cinderella / Anastasia Tremaine** — Drizella's sister under modify memory. Appears as a shorter young woman in bright blue dress with blonde hair. Warns of hidden threats. References the ball incident.
- **Lady Tremaine** — Arrives in a massive palanquin carried by eight people. Controls crow swarms (Nightshade). Claims she can reverse Drizella's aging. Describes herself as "very collaborative these days."
- **Governor Eustus Moro** — Disguised magistrate. Wears fake beard and padded armour. Secretly merciful — uses wax dummy heads for executions. Offers alliance to overthrow Beast King. Wants to free a prisoner named "Trist."

## Lore Reveals

- **Stone Sickness** — Rare condition after petrification. Brain takes ~1 day to reconnect with body. Victim paralysed but conscious.
- **Ring of Objectification** — Transforms people into objects. Normally stops consciousness, but split consciousness (genie nature) causes fractured awareness.
- **Seven Particles Theory** — Consciousness is composed of seven essential particles that can be separated.
- **Phen Language** — Secret noble language used by the aristocracy.
- **Governor Moro's Secret Mercy** — Uses wax dummy heads at executions. Hidden panel system in town hall for letting the condemned escape.

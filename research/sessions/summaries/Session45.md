# Session 45 Summary

**Date:** ~Aug 2025 (Arc 7)
**YouTube:** [Session 45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RonXQDtH-bY)
**Arc:** Arc 7 — Rymes Return / Greenwoods / Grimordia

## Synopsis

**Formal duel: Bigby vs. Henry Borsch** in the Jewelling Hall — a 200-foot arena under anti-magic field with capacity for 100 simultaneous duels on parallel planes. Bigby is equipped with False Life scroll (11 temp HP), Ring of Peerless Apprehension (+3 initiative, extra reaction), and Bracers of the Elements.

Bigby scores a critical hit (38 damage) pinning Henry to the floor. Henry retaliates with Invoke Spirits (summoning a dead opponent from a previous duel) and Stinking Cloud (16 poison damage). The duel is interrupted before full resolution but Henry is effectively defeated.

**Professor Bobins** (necromancer) runs the advanced necromancy course and adjudicates duels. Bigby also gives an architecture master class at the university (30 check, well-received, 17 gold bonus, offered future teaching position by Dean Marikosa).

# Session 35 Summary

**Date:** ~Apr 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 35](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYA2ahLNQuM)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

The **Tournament of the Rising Sun** continues in Pebblecreek. Darian wins the archery competition (150gp prize). Mordred jousts against the Knight of Albore (green armour), losing 4-0 but earning "honour of defeat." **Krennic Silverbarrow** (Duegar, working for the Stewards of Camelot) approaches with a gold offer to purchase the party's magical items. Memphis warned them Krennic would lowball; the Camelot Stewards want to keep powerful items from Avalon.

After the tournament, Mordred meets **Lancelot** — a retired, aged knight living quietly in Pebblecreek. Lancelot invites Mordred for a private discussion and offers to tell him about his father. Mordred is paranoid enough to check whether Lancelot is his brother in disguise (rolls 30, confirms genuine). The party splits: Mordred/Darian go with Lancelot; Drizella/Bigby prepare for a separate meeting with Krennic at 5:30 PM.

## Key Events

- Darian wins archery tournament (150gp)
- Mordred jousts Knight of Albore, loses honourably
- Krennic Silverbarrow offers to buy party's items for the Stewards of Camelot
- Mordred meets Lancelot; invited to private discussion about his father
- Party splits for parallel meetings (Lancelot / Krennic)

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Krennic Silverbarrow** — Duegar steward operative. Offers gold for party's items. Camelot Stewards want to prevent Avalon from acquiring them.
- **Lancelot** — Retired knight in Pebblecreek. Old, living quietly. Recognises something about Mordred. Invites him for private conversation.

## Notable Quotes

- **Mordred** (entering joust): "By the right of my friends."

## Faction Developments

- **Stewards of Camelot** — Actively seeking to buy items to prevent Avalon use. Krennic is their agent.
- **Memphis / Avalon** — Has existing contract with party. Willing to pay more than Stewards.

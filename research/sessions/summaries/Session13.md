# Session 13 Summary

**Date:** ~Jun 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wzndc9Zwd0M)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia

## Synopsis

The session bridges Arc 2 and Arc 3. In the final stretch at Rymes, Sibylla meets with **Agnes Crooked Finger**, who reveals that Nimue has been missing for ~20 years and the coven suspects she is under external control. Agnes gives Sibylla a protective mystery scroll (chalk pattern, bells, brass handles) for use against Nimue. Meanwhile, **Merida** meets **Grandmother Hazel** for witch initiation — Hazel explains three trials: the Trial of the Mind (create a new spell), the Trial of the Heart (use it for good), and the Trial of the Soul (resist corruption from dark magic; those who fail become "hands" — reality-detached beings).

The party arrives in **Oronia**, a coastal city under **Red Hand blockade** (2+ days, searching for a shapeshifter). The Creature must remain at the city outskirts to avoid causing panic. Inside, the party discovers a serial killer has struck 6-7 times over three weeks — victims are arcane practitioners killed by a creature with long bone-like finger claws. The latest victim, **Mr. Poul** (apothecary), is investigated at his shop where the party finds a hidden chamber with a shrine to the Magus (owl symbol), stolen ingredients, and a trail of specialty sea salt leading away from the scene.

Bigby goes undercover at an animal market, infiltrating a secret wizard society led by an elderly wizard. The society believes the murders are an internal power struggle among arcane practitioners, not witch-related. **Dr. Jacob Hilton** appears as the lord's chief consultant on the murders. The session establishes that the murders are likely a false flag to frame the witches, with an aberration (mutagen-created creature) as the weapon.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Dr. Jacob Hilton** — Short brown-haired alchemist/doctor. Lord's chief consultant on the murders. Wears gold-trimmed chain indicating high status. Later revealed as the Devil of Newgate.
- **Lord Edmund** — Oronia's authority figure. Overseeing the murder investigation; directs Bigby to Dr. Hilton.
- **Norman the Doorman** — Young man (~21) with bright green eyes and a moustache. Operates the animal market front for the secret wizard society.
- **The Wise Woman of Oronia** — Young, urban-styled witch. Confirms witches are not responsible for the murders. Notes one deformed witch with an enlarged half-face in the Chantry.
- **Agnes Crooked Finger** — Rymes' wise woman (~97 years old). Gives Sibylla a mystery scroll and warns that the coven may act independently if they encounter Nimue.
- **Mr. Poul** — Apothecary, murder victim #7. Had a hidden shrine to the Magus.

## Notable Quotes

- **Agnes Crooked Finger** (on Nimue): "She has been gone now for near 20 years. I do not think if she were the witch she once was she would not have left all of us in the dark."
- **Grandmother Hazel** (on the Trial of the Soul): "Those who do not [resist] become hands. While they are still welcome in the order, they often find it hard to hold grip to reality and themselves."

## Hero Question

No explicit hero question with alignment choices. Bigby's decision to go undercover alone — without telling the party — served as an implicit trust/agency test.

## Faction Developments

- **Thrice-Triple Covens** — Raise protective barriers on Oronia's Chantry. Proclaim innocence. Concerned about being framed.
- **Red Hand** — Maintaining blockade at Oronia docks. Creating food shortages and public fear.
- **Secret Wizard Society** — Underground group using owl symbol (the Magus). Believes murders are internal arcane power struggle, not witch-related.
- **Lord Edmund's Administration** — Relying on Dr. Hilton as advisor. Initially blamed witches.

## Quest Progress

- **Salasarglumm Contract** — Blocked by Red Hand blockade at Oronia docks. Party needs a ship.
- **Merida's Witch Initiation** — Three trials explained; progress begins.
- **The Creature / Victor Frankenstein** — Creature waits at outskirts. Records of Victor exist in Oronia but location unknown.
- **Serial Murder Investigation** — Evidence points to aberration (mutagen-created), not witches. Dr. Hilton now a key contact (and later the perpetrator).

## Lore Reveals

- **Witch Initiation Trials** — Three trials: Mind (create a spell), Heart (use it for good), Soul (resist dark corruption). Failing the Soul trial creates "hands" — witches disconnected from reality.
- **Aberrations** — Created from mutagens; last days in duration, very powerful. Distinguished from normal creatures by their otherworldly, mutant nature.
- **The Magus** — Arcane deity. Symbol: owl (typically with curved wings). Secret societies use this symbol. Mr. Poul maintained a non-standard shrine.

# Session 30 Summary

**Date:** ~Feb 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DnlIYcvUU3w)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The Beast King's ball and dinner. **Anastasia Tremaine** is revealed as the Beast King's adopted ward — she looks identical to Drizella's deceased sister before her transformation, and is engaged to **Stigen Harrow**. Anastasia shows signs of modified memory: "Memory can be a fickle thing... I have such the feeling I've forgotten something." She urgently tries to contact Drizella, taking a dagger from her dress and raising its hilt toward Drizella as the Beast King escorts her away.

**Cinderella** causes chaos at the ball — dances with Drizella knocking over furniture, appears bloody/singed from an upstairs fire, and attempts to stab Darian with an enchanted pin (17 damage). She references "a mirror and the one who dwells within it" as a threat to Anastasia. **Duke Tyson II** is assassinated by massive magical fire (at least 5th-level) — crown and jewels melted.

Bigby infiltrates the Beast King's study and finds a book with a **Silver Heart symbol** (X mark) containing "rules and advice on how to take over the world" — evidence of Silverheart Court involvement. He also discovers a secret passage behind books leading to the underground **auction house** where the three items will be sold. Members of the **Order of the Eleventh Eye** approach the party at dinner, revealing they've been watching: "You're handling the situation very well."

**King Louie** (a Fey) attends the ball, making cryptic references to "the tree in the swamp." The auction is moving tonight with all three items — an unusual change to the original plan.

## Synopsis

Party arrives at **Everdrift** — King Louie's Summer Court city inside a massive tree. Introduction to four Fae courts: Summer (King Louie), Winter (Lady Sneeden), Spring (Dumbelina), Autumn. The **Tourney of the Four Seasons** begins (wedding/celebration spanning much of Arc 8). Social events and tournament setup. No land-based path to Everdrift; must use the Yellow Road.

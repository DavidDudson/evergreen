# Session 56 Summary

**Date:** ~Dec 2025 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 56](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zTSpjCgFk98)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

Party is *partly dead* — guided by **Glinda** (psychopomp) through Oz. At the Three-Faced Lady's chapel they find beheaded bodies of young, middle-aged, and elderly women — **Morgana killed the Three-Faced Lady and split into three fragments**: Morgana Le Fay, Queen Maeve, and Lady Tremaine.

**Glinda reveals each character's death:**
- Briar: in the arms of her beloved
- Drizella: content at her desk
- Bigby: at the hands of a bitter rival
- **Mordred: very soon, at the hands of his father**

The **Wizard of Oz** (Barnabas Ragdoll) reveals the **Ruby Slippers** are now the **Glass Slippers** of Cindershire. One slipper can undo the Wish Ring that destroyed the druids.

## Key Events

- Morgana killed the Three-Faced Lady; split into three fragments (Morgana, Maeve, Lady Tremaine)
- Glinda reveals each PC's death prophecy
- Wizard of Oz reveals Ruby Slippers = Glass Slippers; can undo Wish Ring
- Briar makes the Fool's deal: returns from death; must sleep in 5 dragon lairs; vomits tongs

## Lore Reveals

- **Three-Faced Lady** — Confirmed dead. Murdered by Morgana. Morgana used the killing to fragment into three selves.
- **Death Prophecies** — Briar (beloved's arms), Drizella (content at desk), Bigby (bitter rival), Mordred (very soon, father's hands).
- **Glass Slippers** — Formerly Ruby Slippers. One can undo the Wish Ring that destroyed the druids.
- **The Fool's Deal** — Briar returns from death. Price: sleep in 5 dragon lairs. Random element: vomits blacksmith's tongs.

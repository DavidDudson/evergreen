# Session 47 Summary

**Date:** ~Sep 2025 (Arc 7)
**YouTube:** [Session 47](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=28wFtc5ee9A)
**Arc:** Arc 7 — Rymes Return / Greenwoods / Grimordia

## Synopsis

Party travels the old Great Trade Road toward Grimordia, encountering a Red Hand blockade led by one-eyed **Alexis Dunos**. They negotiate past, then face the **Cuckoo Witch** in the Darkwoods. Separated from the group, Drizella meets **Bloody Mary** at a mirrored clearing. Mary offers a deal: the **Ring of Peerless Apprehension** (one of Merlin's four treasures) in exchange for safe passage through the Darkwoods. Drizella accepts and also replaces her **eyeball with a mirror shard**, giving Mary permanent scrying ability into Drizella's position. Later, goblins steal the mirror-shard eye as tribute for the **Goblin King**.

## Key Events

- Red Hand blockade on Great Trade Road; party negotiates past Alexis Dunos
- Drizella trades Ring of Peerless Apprehension to Bloody Mary for Darkwoods passage
- Drizella replaces her eyeball with a mirror shard — Mary can see through it
- Goblins steal Drizella's mirror-shard eye as tribute for the Goblin King

## Lore Reveals

- **Great Trade Road** — Connected major Sixth Age glades during the Arrnon Empire.
- **Mirror Shard Eye** — Enables Bloody Mary to scry on Drizella's position permanently.
- **Goblin Territories** — Demand toll/tribute. Goblin King now holds Drizella's eye.

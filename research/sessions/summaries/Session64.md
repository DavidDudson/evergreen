## Synopsis

Mordred has a bad day — Morgana visits his dream, harassing him about weakness. He attempts hostile spell-casting during the dream; Briar wakes him. **Renfield** offers to retrieve horses, revealing he travels by shadow/whispers (bat form) but won't let others join. Bigby detects Renfield's facade — a brief flash of profound cunning before he resumes snivelling. Renfield references overthrowing a "first empress" and won't attend banquets. He keeps his shadow-travel ability secret, fearing the Empress will force him into dangerous tasks.

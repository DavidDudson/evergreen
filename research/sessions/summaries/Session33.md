# Session 33 Summary

**Date:** ~Mar 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 33](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XsKHI2Ul28A)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm (finale)

## Synopsis

The party returns to **Goback** after the Salasarglumm battle. **Mother Gothel** confirms that Morana (Nimue) was an **avatar of the Three-Faced Lady** before Morgana severed her goddess connection, likely using Caliburn. Gothel believes the party are avatars of the old religion. She provides Mordred with an embroidery test for his parentage (magical/extraworld origins).

**Caliburn** has chosen a new keeper — Drizella now wields the sword after Morgana's touch was rejected. The sword "only wants to be wielded by those worthy" who will use it "only when necessary." Caliburn is confirmed as one of three swords (Caliburn, Excalibur, and a third) that together make the original Excalibur. It can destroy evil without killing (turned Beast King into furniture).

The party encounters **Bobby Nos** (a lizard folk woman) who tricks them into a "treasure heist" at **Mother Hubbard's cupboard**. Mother Hubbard is a haggish woman who left the coven order; her cellar contains a **fey trail** passage to the Order of the Eleventh Eye.

The Beast King's journal references "the Rejuvenation" — an upcoming event. The **Glass Slipper** is confirmed as a fey compound between Cinderella and the Fairy Godmother, created by Memphis Cadwallader.

## Key Events

- Mother Gothel confirms Morana was avatar of Three-Faced Lady; Morgana severed the connection using Caliburn
- Caliburn chooses Drizella as new keeper
- Three-sword lore confirmed: Caliburn + Excalibur + third = original Excalibur
- Mother Hubbard's cellar contains a fey trail to the Order of the Eleventh Eye
- Beast King's journal references "the Rejuvenation"
- Glass Slipper confirmed as fey compound created by Memphis Cadwallader
- Party receives gifts from Mother Gothel: Enigma Mirror, Aristotle's Feather Familiar, Staff of the Island
- Combat with multi-legged aquatic creatures in the ruins; party narrowly escapes

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Mother Hubbard** — Old haggish woman, left the coven order. Guards fey trail passage in her cellar. Extensive food stores. Collection of "sharp implements."
- **Bobby Nos** — Lizard folk woman. Trickster who lures party to Mother Hubbard's cupboard under pretence of treasure.

## Notable Quotes

- Hero question: "What are your earliest memories?" — Drizella: father reading to her (safety); Mordred: injured bird, Morgana locked him in room (cruelty); Bigby: playfighting with brother (family); Darian: getting caned for helping someone (injustice).

## Quest Progress

- **Caliburn** — Now with Drizella. Can destroy evil without killing. One of three swords making the original Excalibur.
- **The Rejuvenation** — Unknown event referenced in Beast King's journal. Needs investigation.
- **Order of the Eleventh Eye** — Accessible via fey trail from Mother Hubbard's cellar. Party heading there.
- **Melan** — Person who may know about Caliburn's alien magic origin. To be found.

## Lore Reveals

- **Morana = Avatar of Three-Faced Lady** — Before Morgana severed the connection using Caliburn.
- **Three Swords** — Caliburn, Excalibur, and a third unnamed sword. Together they form the original Excalibur. From "alien/foreign branch of magic."
- **Glass Slipper** — Fey compound between Cinderella and Fairy Godmother, created by Memphis Cadwallader.
- **Caliburn Properties** — Always stays clean. Requires no maintenance. Destroys evil without killing (transforms targets). Only wielded by the worthy.

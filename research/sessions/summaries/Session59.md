# Session 59 Summary

**Date:** ~Feb 2026 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 59](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zM3h_1kbdP4)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

**Mordred summons Morgana** at Everdrift — **three figures appear**: Morgana Le Fay, Queen Maeve, and Lady Tremaine, revealed as fragments of the original Morgana Pendragon. **Medraut** reveals himself (referred to as Mordred's mirror — "my father" context suggests deeper connection). **Robert Tremaine** (Drizella's father) is rescued from mirror imprisonment, appearing aged with white beard. **Nimue**, under magical compulsion, surrenders **Caliburn** to Mordred, briefly saying: "Only the waters of Pendragon can free me." Combat erupts; Morgana fragments and allies escape through portals.

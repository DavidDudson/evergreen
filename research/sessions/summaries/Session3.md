# Session 3 Summary

**Date:** Sep 2023
**YouTube:** [Session 3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z2aL7-MmevY)
**Arc:** Arc 1 — Lowmarsh (Sessions 1–6)

## Synopsis

The session picks up with the party returning to Lowmarsh through the Greenwoods, carrying the captured live ghoul. En route, they encounter **the Green Man** — an ostentatiously dressed Fae figure in green with a cloak of roots and a great cane — who claims ownership of the forest. Merida greets him respectfully in Sylvan, but Drizella insults the Fae creature, prompting him to trap her feet in roots. Mordred pulls her free. Before vanishing behind the trees, the Green Man speaks a single prophetic word to Mordred: **"Sunderance."**

Back in Lowmarsh, the party delivers the ghoul to **Matron Simone** at the Burnt Sage Hospice. Using the captured ghoul, Drizella and Mordred assist with an extraction process — a lumbar-puncture-style procedure to harvest antibodies, producing roughly a litre of curative oil. Matron Simone confirms this is enough to treat the infected, including Bigby. Drizella impresses Simone with her medical knowledge and is granted a **provisional Medicaster licence**, complete with a plague doctor mask (+2 to saves vs. disease/poison).

That evening at the **Lone Pine tavern**, Drizella casts *Waking Nightmare* on Nik Jones as "aversion therapy" for his fear of magic. Nik critically fails his Will save, has a severe panic attack, and involuntarily produces flame from both hands — collaterally disturbing Mordred, who flees to a corner. At **midnight**, a tall cloaked figure in monastic robes appears before Drizella, identifying themselves as one of **the Shadows** (monks serving the Faceless Soldier). The figure warns: *"Something is coming. Many will survive... none unchanged. You do not wish to be here tonight."* The figure then dissolves into shadow.

The next day, the party investigates **Thori Black's quarters** in the treasury building. They discover the entire room is under a **level 8 arcane illusion** — papers contain meaningless filler text, objects flicker between states, and the desk surface shows inconsistent wood grain ("stitching"). The spell was cast approximately one week ago, coinciding with Thori's disappearance. His assistant has also gone missing. The party cannot dispel it themselves and seeks help from the **Immaculate Church of the Goodly Father**, where **Mother Mary Hill** directs them to **Vicar Sean Rothwell** (currently in a committee meeting). The session ends as the party heads to the town hall.

## Faction Developments

- **The Shadic Brethren / The Shadows** — First direct appearance. A monk warns Drizella of danger, identifying them as serving the same master (the Faceless Soldier). They have foreknowledge of catastrophic events and accept casualties ("most will survive"). They dissolve into shadow.
- **The Goodly Father** — The Immaculate Church of the Goodly Father is a major institution in Lowmarsh. Loosely ordered, associated with civilization and law. Mother Mary Hill and Vicar Rothwell lead the local congregation.
- **The Medicasters** — Drizella gains provisional membership. Full accreditation requires visiting the capital. Members receive a plague doctor mask and access to hospital resources.
- **The People's Committee** — Confirmed as a seven-member governing body led by Lewis Short. Will provide monetary reward for the ghoul cure work.

## Quest Progress

- **C1. The Ghoul Fever Cure** — COMPLETED. Antibodies extracted from the captured ghoul at the Burnt Sage Hospice. Cure administered. Matron Simone rewards the party with scrolls and elixirs. Monetary reward from the People's Committee pending.
- **C2. The Thori Black Investigation** — Major progress. His chambers are under a level 8 arcane illusion cast ~1 week ago. His assistant also disappeared. The vault lock is too difficult to pick. Next lead: Vicar Rothwell for magical help, and the People's Committee for information.
- **Officer Bradley's fake heist** — New side job offered. 30 silver per person to stage a fake robbery of the town hall for guard morale. Nik is noncommittal.

## Lore Reveals

- **"Sunderance"** — The Green Man speaks this word to Mordred. This is the first time the word is spoken in the campaign, foreshadowing the central threat.
- **The Faceless Soldier** — Mordred follows this deity and receives waking nightmares as a form of communion. The Faceless Soldier shares a pantheon with the Goodly Father but represents an older, less institutional faith. The scriptures contain the phrase "most will survive."
- **The Shadows / Shadic Brethren** — A secretive monastic order serving the Faceless Soldier. They have advance knowledge of catastrophic events, operate covertly, and literally dissolve into shadow.
- **Level 8 arcane illusion** — Exceptionally rare and powerful. Whoever cast the illusion on Thori Black's chambers is a very powerful arcane practitioner.
- **The Parallax timing** — The dangerous "something" warned about by the Shadow monk appears to be timed to coincide with the Parallax (the winter solstice + full moon alignment), which is now imminent.
- **Lowmarsh geography** — Two main taverns: the Lone Pine and the Split Bell. The Immaculate Church of the Goodly Father is a large building. An underground settlement is accessible via elevator at a location called "Greece" (?), settled partly to escape Fae harassment.

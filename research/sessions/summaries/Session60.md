**Date:** ~Feb 2026 (Arc 8)

## Synopsis

Party arrives in **Oz**, partly dead, guided by **Glinda** (psychopomp). They discover the **ruined chapel of the Three-Faced Lady** — collapsed roof, scorched earth, three-hand symbol on glass. Inside: beheaded corpses (young, middle-aged, elderly women) confirming **Morgana murdered the Three-Faced Lady** and used the killing to split herself into three fragments. An ancient rusted metal figure (frozen in horror pose) stands outside. A **Scarecrow** (Helin) is encountered; quest hook involves Master Tarian (dead 300+ years). Oz lore ties to stopping cyclical deaths (250-300 year fey/mortal cycle).

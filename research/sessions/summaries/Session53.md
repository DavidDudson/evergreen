# Session 53 Summary

**Date:** ~Nov 2025 (transitional)
**YouTube:** [Session 53](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aMkGnS6A8Ls)
**Arc:** Transitional (end of Arc 7 / pre-Arc 8)

## Synopsis

Party arrives in **Sleepy Hollow** after overshooting Everdrift by boat. They land in a graveyard where **Aurora** (Princess of Grimordia) is buried. Graves show their own future deaths. They meet gravedigger Duggan and discover the **Headless Horseman** (a Dullahan — vengeful spirit) hunts the town during the Harvest Festival, taking 11 souls over 11 days. The party is called in on the final night to prevent the last killing. The Harvest Festival cycle repeats every 10 years.

## Key Events

- Party arrives in Sleepy Hollow; finds graves predicting their future deaths
- Headless Horseman (Dullahan) hunts during Harvest Festival — 11 souls over 11 days
- Party tasked with stopping the 11th killing

## Lore Reveals

- **Sleepy Hollow** — Graves predict future deaths. Connected to party via unknown ties.
- **Dullahan** — Vengeful spirit (Headless Horseman). Harvest Festival cycle repeats every 10 years.

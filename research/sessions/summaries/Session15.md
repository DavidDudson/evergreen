# Session 15 Summary

**Date:** ~Jul 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gqOvjB-pmtY)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia

## Synopsis

The session opens with a flashback to **Sibylla's de-aging**: set ~2-3 years after Nimue cast an "eventual spell," Sibylla attempted to cure herself of Red Eye disease using months of alchemical work. The potion backfired catastrophically — de-aging her from a powerful 16th-17th level witch to a 17-year-old with no magical knowledge. Her cat familiar reverted to a newborn kitten. She spent 2-3 years foraging alone in the forest before finding civilisation.

In the present, the party investigates the **Eye of Obaad** — an ancient artifact (the literal eye of an old god, tricked and taken by the Mages). Dame Gothel previously owned it (~50 years ago). Drizella uses her mirror-shard Locate Object ability and finds the Eye aboard the **Good Ship Ragged Muffin** at the south docks, inside a chest protected by a **10th-level Contingency spell** (triggering a 7th-level teleport with ~1,600 km range). The Eye appears alive — it "smiles" at Drizella through the magic.

The party negotiates with the ship's changeling captain, who reveals Camelot's "Sword Test" — all changelings must attempt to pull a sword from a stone during Mayday; failure means forced testing at the capital. The captain sells fake ancestry brands. Merida's natural 20 on diplomacy turns him from neutral to helpful.

**Mother Iron Blood** — a hag working for Morgana (garbled as "Morana" in captions) — attempts to recruit the party to smuggle the Eye to Morgana via the Isle of Flinn. Mordred vehemently refuses: "Morana is not having the Eye." The party touches the chest together, triggering the Contingency — Mother Iron Blood appears floating above the ship, demanding they surrender the Eye. The session ends with combat initiated.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Mother Iron Blood** — Hag working for Morgana. Wears purple, twisted/crooked hands. Uses Mirror Scout illusion to vanish. Attempted to recruit the party at the Chantry via tea invitation. Ultimatum: surrender the Eye or fight.
- **The Ragged Muffin Captain** — Changeling/hex blood ship captain. Yellow eyes, scales on fingers, cigarette holder. Deals in stolen goods and fake ancestry brands. Turned helpful via Merida's nat 20 diplomacy.
- **Horus and Jasper** — Dock thugs guarding the Ragged Muffin. Easily swayed by diplomacy/bribery.
- **Moonbeam** (Moonam) — Wise woman of the Oronia Chantry. Directs the party to Alexander Dunos; acts coy about the Eye.

## Lore Reveals

- **Eye of Obaad** — Literal eye of an old god. The god was tricked by the Mages, who took the eye. Dame Gothel owned it ~50 years ago. It is alive, can see, and smiles. Powers relate to "ornamental and ceremonial" magic (per Sibylla).
- **Sibylla's True Nature** — Was once a child-eating hag. Used alchemy to de-age and escape Red Eye disease, losing all power. Currently on a redemption arc.
- **Red Eye Disease** — Incurable plague. Causes blood shock in eyes, turns victims into mindless aggressive ghouls. Common in the north.
- **Changeling Sword Test** — Camelot mandatory test (~2-3 years old). All changelings must attempt to pull sword from stone during Mayday. Success = brand and freedom. Failure = forced testing.
- **10th-Level Contingency** — The chest's protection. Triggers a 7th-level spell (teleport) with ~22,000 ft / 1,600 km range. Someone powerful set this trap.

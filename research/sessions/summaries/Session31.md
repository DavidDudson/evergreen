# Session 31 Summary

**Date:** ~Feb 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9578iPqvnVA)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The ball erupts into full combat. The party has stolen the artifacts (Looking Glass, Staff of Beguiling, Ring of Polymorph) and the Beast King takes flight — hovering 95 feet above the palace. The folk of the glade are rebelling, armed with pitchforks, torches, and sickles, building scarecrow effigies of the Beast King. **Kristoff Marlo** arrives dramatically via waterfall teleportation on a turtle mount with 10 doves, bringing counter-magic against the Beast King's healing.

Bigby transforms into beast form via the Ring of Polymorph and attempts to grapple Carlos. Drizella casts **Warriors Regret** on the Beast King — a curse that forces visions of his victims (bones at the bottom of the palace pond) and deals damage when he attacks. She also uses Locate Object to track **Cinderella**, who is independently destroying the palace with Hellfire. Mordred uses Message + Diplomacy combos to weaken the Beast King's will saves and attempts to turn **Atasha** (the sphinx ally) — rolling 31 on diplomacy — but Atasha refuses, fearing the commoner mob more than the party.

**Darian** is critically wounded by the Beast King (crushed mid-air, falls to Dying 2) and mysteriously transported to a **desert landscape with moving stars** — a separate location/plane entirely. He stabilises but is completely separated from the party.

The Beast King is Frightened 2 and damaged but still flying. The fight continues.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Kristoff Marlo** — Wizard. Teleports in via waterfall on a turtle mount with 10 doves. Has counter-magic to stop the Beast King's healing.
- **Atasha** — Sphinx ally of the Beast King. Conflicted. Refuses to switch sides because she fears the commoner mob will destroy the Glade. Casts hostile magic after rejection.

## Notable Quotes

- **Drizella** (on Cinderella): "She was mine before she fell under your protection."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question. Unresolved mystery: why was Darian transported to a desert with moving stars?

## Faction Developments

- **Commoner Rebellion** — Folk of the Glade armed and rebelling. Building scarecrow effigies. Throwing rocks at Atasha. Della briefly commands them.
- **Atasha** — Refuses to betray the Beast King. Her loyalty is to the Glade itself, not Carlos. Fears mob destruction more than tyranny.
- **Cinderella** — Operating independently with Hellfire, destroying the palace. Not aligned with the Beast King despite his claims of "protection."

## Quest Progress

- **Three Items** — Retrieved (Looking Glass, Staff of Beguiling, Ring of Polymorph). Beast King fight ongoing.
- **Beast King** — Frightened 2, cursed with Warriors Regret, but still flying. Marlo has counter-healing magic.
- **Darian** — Separated from party, wounded, in unknown desert location with moving stars.
- **Cinderella** — Tracked by Locate Object. Burning the palace independently.

## Lore Reveals

- **Warriors Regret** — Curse that forces visions of victims. Deals d8 damage per creature the target intentionally damages that turn. Lasts until target spends a full round without attacking.
- **Beast King's Victims** — Bones visible at the bottom of the palace pond. Revealed through the curse's visions.
- **Darian's Desert** — Mysterious plane with moving stars and sand. Separate from the palace. Method of transport unknown.

# Session 18 Summary

**Date:** ~Sep 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F6ZdfZQgJ9s)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia (finale)

## Synopsis

The session opens with character backstory sharing: Sibylla was pursued by witch hunters in the woods; Mordred's entire woodland childhood was traumatic (he witnessed dead Camelot soldiers eaten by ravens); Drizella tried to help a lost child after the ball but the child screamed and ran, breaking her belief she could rejoin society; Bigby was suspected of murder at the Hogson Brothers' place and hunted by the Huntsman.

The party meets **Billy Bones** at Auntie Scones tavern in Oronia. He gives them an introduction letter and a **two-part treasure map** (requires holding up to light with a companion piece) for Captain Smelt of the **Hispaniola**. The party signs aboard: Drizella as cook's assistant, Sibylla as navigator assistant, Bigby helping with navigation, Mordred as general deckhand.

Below deck they discover a **mysterious prisoner** — a plant-humanoid creature covered in magical vines and thorns, chanting "As Above So Below" repeatedly. It was found at an altar surrounded by dead bodies and thick briars, and has resisted all interrogation (ten fingers removed, wizards tried and failed). A jailer guards the cage.

Mordred's naive generosity causes chaos when he gives 2 silver pieces to every crew member (2 weeks' wages), making the crew suspicious that something dodgy is happening. **Squire John Trone** lectures Mordred about wealth ethics.

Mordred casts bone-reading to foresee the ship's future and sees it **breaking in half and sinking**. Pirates attack — green signal flashes, a ship approaching from the south. The main mast falls and pins **Captain Smelt** to the stairs; Sibylla lifts it with telekinesis. A fierce boarding battle follows; the party repels the pirates through spell coordination. Below deck, **Billy Mo** (Billy Bones' associate) is found stabbed, gasping his last words: **"Beware a one-legged fiend."**

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Billy Bones** — Drunken sailor at Auntie Scones. Provides introduction to Captain Smelt and a two-part treasure map.
- **Captain Alexander Smelt** — Refined, experienced captain of the Hispaniola. Served three legs (?) in Camelot. No-alcohol policy. Keeps the destination secret. Badly wounded when mast falls on him.
- **Mr. Arrow** — First Mate. Stern, professional, military bearing. Dragon and naval tattoos. Takes command when Captain is wounded.
- **Dr. David Livesley** — Scholar navigator aboard the Hispaniola. Studies medical texts and sea routes.
- **Pip** — Ship's cook. Tattooed. Has a pig named Bacon that eats pickles.
- **Jim** — Young cabin boy (~13-14). Excited about first voyage. Befriends Mordred.
- **Squire John Trone** — Knight with lion fur cape. Financial expert. Lectures Mordred about wealth and class.
- **Fox** — Mysterious wealthy traveler smoking pink smoke. Heading to Port Six.
- **Billy Mo** — Billy Bones' associate. Found murdered below deck. Last words: "Beware a one-legged fiend."
- **The Plant Prisoner** — Green, vine-covered humanoid chanting "As Above So Below." Found at an altar surrounded by dead bodies and briars. Resists all interrogation.

## Lore Reveals

- **The White Tower** — A major structure in the South that has fallen. Significance not yet explained but treated as urgent.
- **"As Above So Below"** — Chanted by the plant prisoner. Later connected to the Horned King's Not-Deer manifestations and the Blight plague. First in-campaign instance of this phrase from a living source.
- **Camelot Military** — Gold dragons with spears represent Camelot military. War ended ~5-15 years ago. Veterans now appearing as pirates.
- **Treasure Map** — Two-part design requiring a companion piece. Billy Bones entrusted it to Captain Smelt. Leads to an unknown location (palm trees, abstract geography, possibly an island).
- **Plant-Humanoid Creatures** — Found at altars surrounded by dead bodies and briars. Resistant to torture and magical interrogation. Connected to the antiseed/Horned King mythology.

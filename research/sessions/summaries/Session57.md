# Session 57 Summary

**Date:** ~Jan 2026 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 57](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a-9AEWVII3k)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

Mordred uses **Clarent/Caledfwlch** as a magical key in the **Mirror of Aretas** to free **Bloody Mary**. The sword unlocks two of three locks on her divine/religious prison. Mary — revealed as either a ghost or astral being — is freed and grants boons to the party. A **dark deer with white shining eyes** appears at the mirror's back, ominous and foreshadowing the Horned King's return.

## Key Events

- Bloody Mary freed from the Mirror of Aretas using Clarent/Caledfwlch as key
- Mary grants boons upon liberation
- Dark deer with white eyes appears (Horned King harbinger)
- Mary's nature clarified: ghost/astral being, bound by divine locks

## Lore Reveals

- **Clarent/Caledfwlch** — Functions as magical key to the Mirror of Aretas. Same metal as the mirror.
- **Dark Deer** — White-eyed deer appears when Mary is freed. Harbinger of the Horned King's return.

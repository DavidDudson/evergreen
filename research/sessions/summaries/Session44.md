# Session 44 Summary

**Date:** ~Aug 2025 (Arc 7)
**YouTube:** [Session 44](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p1jOSv9PqF4)
**Arc:** Arc 7 — Rymes Return / Greenwoods / Grimordia

## Synopsis

University downtime at Ersatz University (~30 days). Party enrols in courses: Drizella in Pottery Law (art); Bigby in Contract Law (highest-level fey contract course); Briar Rose in Sun Magic Theory + Sleep/Dream Enchantment; Mordred in Dream Interpretation + Counselling.

**Dean Marikosa** reveals herself as a **3rd-age soul mage** who achieved immortality by separating soul from body. She sustains herself on 18% of ambient soul fragments naturally shed by ~80 students annually. **Merlin's binding** to the Pendragon line dates to the 4th age — **Varos** (founder of the Northern Kingdom, Arrnon Empire conspirator) made the original pact.

The undead plague investigation narrows to three suspects. **Henry Borsch** (wealthy Lord Borsch's son) is the primary suspect; he kills Briar's familiar **Bud** with a toxic cloud breath during stealth reconnaissance. Session ends with a duel challenge.

## Key Events

- Party enrols in university courses (~30 day downtime)
- Dean Marikosa reveals: 3rd-age soul mage, immortal via soul separation
- Souls naturally shed 18% over 80-100 year lifespan; ghosts/fey feed on fragments
- Merlin's binding dates to 4th age; Varos made the original pact
- Henry Borsch identified as undead plague suspect; kills Briar's familiar Bud
- Duel challenge issued against Henry Borsch

## Lore Reveals

- **Soul Mechanics** — Souls shed 18% over a lifespan. Ghosts and fey feed on fragments. Dean Marikosa recycles ambient fragments for immortality.
- **Avatars** — Walking gods. Don't shed soul. Have dual souls. Appear every few generations.
- **Varos/Vavalyon** — Arrnon Empire conspirator. Merlin's benefactor. Founder of Camelot's predecessor. Made the original binding pact.

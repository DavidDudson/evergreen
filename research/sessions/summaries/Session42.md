# Session 42 Summary

**Date:** ~Jul 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NheM1MD4DTc)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

The party discovers a massive greenhouse in the Sun Spire containing **100+ antiseeds in a stagnant fountain** and Nimue's sealed laboratory where she conducted antiseed research. Inside a cocoon of thorny briars, they find **Briar Rose (Aurora)** in enchanted sleep. The party wakes her via a blood ritual at the fountain — her 21st birthday triggers the awakening, protected by ancient fey magic.

Nimue appears outside the greenhouse window in drowned-lady water form and attacks with animated servants and water powers. Combat ensues; the party defeats her servants. Upon breaking Nimue's control, the greenhouse transforms — old magic cleansed, becoming beautiful and green again.

Nimue's laboratory notes reveal she has been systematically harvesting and studying antiseeds, documenting extensive intel on the party and their allies. Antiseeds are confirmed as **hybrid soul parasites** that feed on the host's life force while keeping them alive.

## Key Events

- 100+ juvenile antiseeds discovered in greenhouse fountain
- Briar Rose (Aurora) found sleeping in cocoon of thorny briars; woken via blood ritual
- Nimue appears in water form and attacks; party defeats her servants
- Greenhouse transforms beautiful upon breaking Nimue's control
- Nimue's laboratory contains antiseed research notes and intel on the party

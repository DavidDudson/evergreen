## Synopsis

The session opens with a recap of character secrets — each PC has a hidden trait the others might notice. Drizella disappears most evenings to pray but returns shaky and disturbed; Bigby fidgets with poppets; something in the air around Mordred is subtly off (Sibylla would notice); and other hints are shared.

The party storms the Ersatz University Observatory, where Nimue (the skull-faced witch previously seen harvesting organs at Lowmarsh) is conducting a divination ritual during the Parallax — a rare moment when divination magic is at peak power. She has mind-controlled Savvan and is using him to operate an elaborate alchemical machine, attempting to determine when the Sunderance will occur. Dean Marikosa is found tied up in a back chamber.

Combat is brutal. Nimue casts an 8th-level dominate spell (DC 40) on Mordred, turning him against the party. Drizella is critically wounded and enters the space between life and death, where the Reaper appears — the same deity whose Blessing they earned at the Bonekeeper's Guild. The Reaper offers to let her wait rather than pass on. While dying, Drizella receives a telepathic message from Nimue's true self — a peaceful, drowning figure trapped within the corrupted shell — who reveals: "The Waters of Pendragon can free me."

The party destroys the divination machine. Nimue escapes into the sky, but not before taking Drizella's mask. In the wreckage, Sibylla finds an ornate gold-tipped scabbard — identified through occultism as the scabbard of Caliburn, one of the three sister swords (alongside Excalibur and a third unnamed blade). Merlin gifted Caliburn to Nimue after the Strife of Camlin, but the sword itself is missing.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Nimue** (the skull-faced witch) — Fully confronted for the first time. Revealed as the Lady of the Lake who held Caliburn. Has two aspects: a corrupted, green-eyed form and a trapped, peaceful true self. Fled the Covenant after her student Morgana went rogue.
- **Savvan** — The changeling friend from Lar, now mind-controlled by Nimue to operate the divination machine. Fights the control and apologises profusely once freed.
- **Dean Marikosa** — Found beaten and bound in the Observatory's back chamber. Rescued by the party.
- **The Reaper** — Appears to dying Drizella in the liminal space. Skeletal, cloaked, carries a scythe. Honours her service to the Bonekeeper's Guild and offers her more time among the living.

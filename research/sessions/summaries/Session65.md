# Session 65 Summary

**Date:** ~Mar 2026 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 65](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Hxet7fj2-pM)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

Circus battle. Party fights Ring Master, Strongman, and acrobat in a massive travelling circus. Briar and her summoned creature Buddy both die when Briar touches an artifact orb during a time-stop bubble. **The Fool** (god of death/joy, jester persona) manifests and offers a rock-paper-scissors game for survival. Briar loses and dies permanently (confetti). The rest of the party continues fighting. Drizella deals heavy damage with a dragon companion. Bigby makes invisible sneak attacks. New enemies appear: mime Bianco and street magician Zambini.

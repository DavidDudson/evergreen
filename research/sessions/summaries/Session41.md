# Session 41 Summary

**Date:** ~Jul 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 41](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xPOXJ582U2M)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

Full dock combat. **Lady Elizabeth Pikes** and **Peter Hogson** arrive to retrieve an iron chest from the docks. The Order of the Eleventh Eye arrives on an enlarged owl-bear for support. **Ida Montague** is revealed as **undead** — Darian's volcanic fissure one-shots her initially but she rises again. The undead sailors are construct-undead hybrids immune to Harm spells (which heal them instead).

Mordred touches **Caliburn** to the chest — a massive green light explosion visible across the city forms a **silver dragon silhouette**. Inside: an **antiseed device** tied to Ida Montague's heart (she's the vessel). In a dream/spirit space, Mordred sees Ida's heart — magically enormous, suspended in space — and realises it was once a person. He communicates with the heart: "They made me like this." He uses Celestial Accord to shift Ida from hostile to neutral.

**Lady Pikes** is also undead, connected to Ida's heart via the dream realm. Mordred succeeds a natural 20 Will save to force Pikes out of the dream space. She flees.

**Darian Sand reaches Dying 3** — paralysed by Peter Hogson (nat 1 fortitude save), dragged away by sailors. In a spirit realm, he sees party members as coloured auras. One more failure = death.

# Session 20 Summary

**Date:** ~Oct 2024 (Arc 4)
**YouTube:** [Session 20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-r2dwnCAqnE)
**Arc:** Arc 4 — Sea / Jolly Roger / Goback

## Synopsis

Chaos erupts aboard the Hispaniola. Sixteen mutineers move through the ship while the **elf prisoner** breaks free from its cage in an explosion of vines and growth. Drizella uses a mirror to stun the elf (it freezes upon seeing its own reflection), then extracts an **Antiseed** from its chest using surgical acupuncture — a dark, seed-like object of blighted primordial corruption. The elf dies as the vines wither.

The party finds two map pieces in the Captain's cabin. Held together under light, they reveal a **Treasure Island** with navigation instructions: Elephant's Rock, Cross Palm Trees, Hanging Sand. The Captain gives the map and apologises. Mordred holds the Antiseed and receives a vision: the great tree of Arcadia plummeting into water, cracking, rotting, engulfed in fire. A presence speaks: "I've given you my name, tell me yours... there is something familiar in your blood."

The Hispaniola's helmsman suddenly goes rigid with terror as the waves "all go glassy" — then jumps to his death. Moments later, a **colossal flying warship** appears: the **Jolly Roger**, four times the Hispaniola's size, seven levels, hundreds of pirates. The party is captured and brought aboard. **Captain Hook** — tall, red coat, feathered hat, metal hook — gives them two options: shake his hand (join crew to next stop) or grip his hook (harsher terms). They shake his hand and agree to two days' work until they reach the White Shore, then Goback.

Below deck in Hook's forbidden room, the party discovers **Mor'du** — a gargantuan crocodile connected to brass wires, powering the entire ship via magical tether. Surrounding Mor'du are glass cages containing tiny glowing fey/fairies, goblin-type creatures bound with brass wires, and a **blighted five-headed creature** (black ooze, red eye) that says "I will eat that skin." A **scabbard matching Nimue's** is found in the room. The session ends with the party debating whether to free the creatures (crashing the ship in ~1 hour) or wait until port.

- Fey creatures imprisoned in glass cages; a blighted five-headed demon creature also caged
- Scabbard matching Nimue's found in the forbidden room
- Party debates freeing the creatures vs. waiting for port

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Captain Hook** — Tall, red coat, feathered hat, metal hook for right hand. Charismatic, theatrical pirate captain of the flying Jolly Roger. Allows party to leave at the White Shore in two days. Mentions Nick Jones. Rules: no brawling, no stealing, don't enter his cabin or the forbidden room.
- **Mor'du** (Beast of the Shallows) — Gargantuan crocodile connected to brass wires, powering the Jolly Roger. In extreme pain. Previously missing from known locations.
- **The Blighted Creature** — Five-headed, black ooze with red eye, caged in the forbidden room. Says: "I will eat that skin."

## Notable Quotes

- **The Antiseed presence** (to Mordred): "I've given you my name, tell me yours... there is something familiar in your blood."
- **Drizella** (on the Antiseed): "This looks like something from the Darkwoods but it's even more corrupt than anything I've ever seen before."
- **Captain Hook:** "Mutineers died a mutineer's death."
- **The blighted creature:** "I will eat that skin."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question. The session's central moral choice: free the imprisoned creatures now (risking the ship crashing) or wait until port (allowing their suffering to continue). Unresolved at session's end.

## Faction Developments

- **Captain Hook's Pirates** — Major faction revealed. Hundreds of crew, well-organised, flying warship. Hook is charismatic but ruthless. Ship powered by imprisoned creatures.
- **Darkwoods / Primordial Powers** — Antiseed confirmed as primordial corruption from the Darkwoods. Not human, fey, or arcane magic. Connected to Arcadia's fall.
- **Hispaniola Crew** — Effectively destroyed. Mutineers killed. Captain injured. Ship left drifting or taken aboard the Jolly Roger.

## Quest Progress

- **Antiseed** — First one extracted from a living host. Drizella's surgery succeeded. Mordred now carries it and has made contact with the presence within.
- **Treasure Map** — Combined and decoded. Navigation: Elephant's Rock, Cross Palm Trees, Hanging Sand.
- **Mor'du** — Found powering the Jolly Roger. Party considering freeing it.
- **Scabbard** — Matching Nimue's, found in Hook's forbidden room. Connection unclear.
- **Goback** — Party heading there after the White Shore (2 days). Hook says "rockus happened" there.

## Lore Reveals

- **Antiseed Nature** — Primordial corruption from the Darkwoods. More corrupt than anything previously seen. Contains a presence that recognises Mordred's Pendragon bloodline. Shows visions of Arcadia's destruction.
- **Mor'du / Beast of the Shallows** — Gargantuan crocodile, previously missing. Now imprisoned aboard the Jolly Roger, connected via brass wires to power the flying ship.
- **The Jolly Roger** — Captain Hook's flying warship. Powered by imprisoned magical creatures via brass-wire tethers. 4x the size of the Hispaniola, 7+ levels, hundreds of crew. Pink stained-glass windows on Hook's cabin.
- **Arcadia Vision** — The great tree of Arcadia plummeting into water, cracking, rotting, engulfed in fire. Connected to the Antiseed's primordial memory.

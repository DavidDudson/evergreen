# Session 28 Summary

**Date:** ~Jan 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8s_IpM80cjc)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The party investigates the **Fumus Tower ruins** north of Salasarglumm, where people have been mysteriously disappearing. At the centre they find a **willow tree with a magical dagger** (runic handle, opal gem, waved blade) stabbed through its heart, surrounded by purified salt. Two **Dryad sisters** (humanoid plant creatures with wooden claws, glowing green eyes, horns of wood) guard the tree — an Awakened Fey entity predating the Iran dynasty. One sister has been wounded by the dagger; the other is chained in cold iron in the basement of **Baron Lumella's wizard tower** in Barrow.

The tree has been killing people indiscriminately because its network is severed — the wizard (Lumella or his predecessor) stabbed its heart and enslaved one sister. Lumella, who trained under Kristoff Marlo (disappeared years ago) and then under Atasha (the Beast King's advisor), has been experimenting on the captive Dryad. The party discovers a **Looking Glass with a 4th-level confusion enchantment** in the tower's meeting room.

Mordred's **magical black gauntlet** is confirmed as a cursed item ("doomed by the Narrative"). When activated: +1 AC but spreads dark purple-black corruption up his arm. Cannot be removed (DC 30). Mordred sends a dream message to Morgana. The party prepares for the Beast King's 5-year anniversary ball, planning to free **Tristan Iran** (the imprisoned heir) publicly to spark a revolution.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Dryad Sisters** (x2) — Awakened Fey entities predating the Iran dynasty. Humanoid plant creatures. One guards the stabbed tree; the other is chained in cold iron underground. Willing to make pacts if freed.
- **Baron Lumella** — Wizard trained by Kristoff Marlo, then Atasha. Father was a lumber worker. Obsessed with destroying the Darkwoods. Lying about his magical practices. Enslaved one Dryad.
- **Tristan Iran** — Imprisoned heir of the Iran dynasty. Caged in Iran Square. Uncle Garen organising support for his liberation.

## Lore Reveals

- **Cold Iron** — Deadly to fey. Used to chain the Dryad sister.
- **Fey Pacts** — Sealed by drinking tree sap. Establishes magical connection.
- **Awakened Trees** — Can live centuries, possess sentience. Predates current political structures.
- **Salt Circles** — Purified salt creates magical barriers that burn/sting fey. Particularly effective against fey magic.
- **Mordred's Gauntlet Curse** — "Doomed by the Narrative." +1 AC on activation but dark corruption spreads through the body. 9th-rank magic. Cannot be removed (DC 30).

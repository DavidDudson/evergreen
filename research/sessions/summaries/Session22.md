# Session 22 Summary

**Date:** ~Nov 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 22](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Od8doiW5nTQ)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The session bridges Arc 4 and Arc 5. At Goback's ziggurat, the party experiences intense visions: Drizella sees dead corpses animated by vines/thorns and many pairs of pink eyes in darkness; Mordred sees a metallic landscape with a bleeding anvil, a golden dragon falling, and 10,000 crows chanting "blood, blood, blood"; Darian sees a hooded figure in a desert with one good eye (other socket empty) who speaks of sacrifice, plus symbols — a rusting sword, crown, dead tree, three swords, 12 stars in a circle, and many skulls.

On the road to Salasarglumm, the party encounters **Dame Gothel** at her cottage. She confirms the three items are with Carlos (the Beast King) and provides critical intelligence: Carlos has an **Antiseed growing inside him** but is too crude to become the Horned King — though if left unchecked for 10 years, it will kill him. The **Ring of Objectification** is Gothel's own creation, invisible to Carlos, and she wants it recovered above all. She warns about the **Silverheart Court** conspiracy and advises winning over or eliminating **Baron Drazag** to spark a revolt.

A woman named **Morgana** (appearing ~45-50 years old, kind eyes) is present at Gothel's cottage. She reveals herself as Bigby's aunt — not his mother, but his mother's twin sister, created ~25 years ago in a failed experiment. She shows a magnificent sword (golden handle with rubies, forged from shattered sword shards welded together) and claims Morgana has been seeking someone to carry Excalibur.

**Darian Sand** formally joins the party after emerging from mirror-rift portals. He experienced interdimensional travel through a mirror network, seeing visions of a man with a hook hand, a tall veiled woman, and other scenes.

The party approaches Salasarglumm's western gatehouse and is ambushed by plague-infected **goblins** riding rat-dog hybrids. Mordred is bitten and infected with **Goblin Pox** (second stage). Combat is ongoing at session's end.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Dame Gothel** — Wise witch in a cottage near Salasarglumm. Kind demeanour, summons doves. Created the Ring of Objectification. Warns about Carlos's Antiseed and the Silverheart Court. Asks party to prioritise recovering the Ring.
- **Morgana** (at Gothel's) — Woman ~45-50 years old. Bigby's aunt (mother's twin sister), created ~25 years ago in a failed experiment. Shows Excalibur. Kind eyes, no bitterness.
- **Nim** — Apprentice/ward of Gothel. Has "strayed from her path."
- **Atasha** — Local advisor/diplomat in the Salasarglumm region. Sister of the chapel's matron mother. Has served Baron Drazag.
- **Carlos the Naf** (Beast King) — Wizard who cursed himself with animal form. Stole the three items from Memphis. Killed Salasarglumm's previous king, married his daughter. Has an Antiseed growing in his chest.

## Notable Quotes

- **Desert oracle** (to Darian): "All knowledge requires sacrifice. You have begun the sacrifice. Finish it and you shall receive what you seek."
- **Dame Gothel:** "There is another reason I would see him off the throne. A great injustice has been done in this place. Carlos came here, killed the King, married his daughter, and has plunged the land into despair."
- **Morgana** (to Bigby): "I am not who you think I am. My name is Morgana. I am not your mother."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question with alignment choices. Moral tension around whether to help spark a revolt vs. just stealing items and leaving.

## Faction Developments

- **Silverheart Court** — Confirmed as conspiracy involving Queen Maeve, the Hogson Brothers, merchant factions, and possibly the Red Hand. Memphis Cadwallader suspected as central figure.
- **Dame Gothel** — Alive and active near Salasarglumm. Created the Ring of Objectification. Allied with the party.
- **Beast King's Rule** — Carlos killed the previous king, married his daughter, rules through Baron Drazag and guards.

## Quest Progress

- **Three Items Contract** — Party approaching Salasarglumm. Gothel advises winning over Baron Drazag or sparking revolt.
- **Antiseed Threat** — Carlos has one growing in him. Too crude to become Horned King but will die in 10 years if unchecked.
- **Excalibur** — Shown by Morgana. Forged from shattered sword shards. Seeking a worthy bearer.
- **Goblin Pox** — Mordred infected (second stage). Needs treatment.

## Lore Reveals

- **12 Stars of Power** — The Staff of Beguiling is one of 12 ancient powerful artifacts (12 stars in a circle seen in Darian's vision).
- **Antiseed Mechanics** — Seeds from Arcadia's ancient tree. Corrupt the host, twist the soul. If an Antiseed grows in the "right/wrong person," it could create the Horned King. Carlos's is growing but he's too crude to trigger the transformation.
- **Ring of Objectification** — Created by Dame Gothel. Plain gold wedding band. Allows transformation of people to objects and vice versa. Invisible to Carlos.
- **Mirror Network** — Interdimensional system of mirrors/rifts. Guided by an unknown entity (possibly the Looking Glass). Too dangerous for mortal use.
- **Pink Eyes as Marker** — Characters with pink eyes may be corrupted or touched by dark power: Morgana Le Fay, Bigby's mother, and one more the party will meet.
- **Excalibur** — Golden handle with rubies, made from shattered sword shards welded together. Morgana has been searching for someone worthy to wield it.

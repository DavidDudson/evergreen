# Session 16 Summary

**Date:** ~Jul 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gqxo2tnzOok)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia

## Synopsis

The session opens with a **Drizella flashback** set seven years before the present. Lady Tremaine, having married into respectability, promised her daughters they wouldn't have to work — but broke that promise, forcing Drizella and Anastasia to labour at a shoe boutique. A ball is announced by the Lord of Cindershire; during it, Drizella undergoes a mysterious transformation — an itching, creeping sensation across her arm and back that leaves her altered. Cinderella appears at the ball magically transformed in an iridescent gold dress and glass shoes.

In the present, the party has defeated **Freya Iron Blood** (Mother Iron Blood) on the Ragged Muffin. Alexander, her minion, explodes into ash. The **Eye of Obaad** is lost — dropped into the water during the fight, sunk 25+ feet, presumably unrecoverable. The Red Hand, satisfied with the resolution, lifts the blockade and rewards the party with magical runes (Striking, Reinforcing, Crushing).

The party is tasked by Lord Edmund / the Red Hand Preacher to investigate **necromancer activity** in the city (burned bodies, sulfur/honey residue) and find **Victor Frankenstein**. They locate Victor's lodgings and arrive before the Creature. **Elizabeth** (Victor's wife/associate, with an infant) answers the door and praises Victor's kindness. The Creature arrives mid-conversation and goes berserk: "Why are they good enough but why was I not good enough?" Combat ensues in Victor's home. Sibylla makes Elizabeth invisible to protect her. The party attempts de-escalation (Calm spells, reasoning) rather than lethal force. Darian discovers Frankenstein's workshop in a side room — body parts, a mechanical constructor, and a cellar door. The session ends mid-combat as the Creature heads toward the cellar.

## Key Events

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Elizabeth** — Woman in Victor Frankenstein's household (wife/assistant). Has an infant child. Praises Victor's cleverness and kindness. Made invisible by Sibylla during the Creature's attack.
- **Cinderella (Ella)** — Appears in Drizella's flashback. Adopted sister. Arrives at the ball magically transformed in gold dress and glass shoes.
- **Anastasia** — Drizella's biological sister. Dark hair, green eyes, pale. Appears in flashback.
- **Galahad** — Referenced as Mordred's only childhood friend. Wanted to join Knights of Camelot while Mordred had to flee from Morgana.

- **Eye of Obaad** — Lost at sea. Neither the witches, Morgana, nor the Red Hand possess it.
- **Camelot / Avalon** — Major political lore dump: Camelot effectively leaderless (Arthur has no heir; someone attacked Pendragon Castle 15-16 years ago). Avalon exhausted by 100+ years of war. Neutral lords withdrawing support from both sides.

## Lore Reveals

- **Camelot** — Capital is Pendragon on Uther Lake. King Arthur rules but has no heir (~30 years). Someone attacked Pendragon Castle 15-16 years ago, possibly killing the heir. Council holding things together.
- **Avalon** — Capital is Aspenmarch (?). Ruled by Queen Maeve (~30 years). Originally governed by dwarven barons exiled from underground. Queen Maeve killed the barons with help. Her daughter fled overseas to the "Jarish Coast." At war with Camelot for ~100 years.
- **Morgana Le Fay** — Resides in Camelot territory. Mordred escaped from her. Not confirmed associated with Avalon.
- **Galahad** — Named after legendary Knights of the Round Table. Mordred's only friend during training. Joined the Knights while Mordred fled.
- **Victor Frankenstein** — Respected surgeon with university backing. Created the Creature through non-arcane, non-divine magic (unknown type). Has a workshop with body parts and mechanical apparatus.

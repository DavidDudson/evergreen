# Session 43 Summary

**Date:** ~Jul 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 43](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pof4o4PAYEk)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek (finale)

## Synopsis

Party departs Pebblecreek aboard the **Proud Mary** with **Clara Murse** and **Lancelot**. Red Hand agents are observed on the ship. Nimue attacks the party with 5-6 **spectral eyes** (manifestations of her divided consciousness). Violent naval combat nearly kills Lancelot. The party defeats Nimue's manifestations; she retreats with Darian's body. Nimue has 6 major antiseeds remaining (destroyed 1 in Session 42, started with 7).

**Queen Titania** meets the party and bestows **four sacred items left by Merlin**: a draconic egg, an alabaster key (planar travel device), and two symbolic artifacts. These are "essential to saving him." Titania confirms Merlin's final words: **"Forgive me, Sister"** — revealing Titania is Merlin's sister and guardian of his relics.

## Key Events

- Party sails from Pebblecreek on the Proud Mary
- Nimue attacks with spectral eye manifestations; retreats with Darian's body
- Lancelot nearly killed in naval combat
- Queen Titania bestows Merlin's four sacred items (draconic egg, alabaster key, two artifacts)
- Titania confirmed as Merlin's sister; his last words: "Forgive me, Sister"

- Astius heading to Avalon to await party contact

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Clara Murse** — Dead Moon pilgrim. Traveling with party. Seeking relic south of Ilix Ridge.

## Lore Reveals

- **Titania = Merlin's Sister** — Guardian of his relics. His last words to her: "Forgive me, Sister."
- **Merlin's Four Items** — Draconic egg, alabaster key (planar travel), two symbolic artifacts. Essential for saving Merlin from dimensional imprisonment.
- **Nimue's Antiseeds** — 6 major antiseeds remaining. She also has Darian's body.
- **Red Hand** — Establishing presence on merchant river routes. Religious expansion into northern territories.

# Session 32 Summary

**Date:** ~Mar 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 32](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tr-0pQcAg-U)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

**Morana** (the Lady of the Lake / Black Raven figure) arrives at the Salasarglumm Glade demanding Caliburn and the Beast King's life. A tense standoff escalates into combat. Morana attempts to seize Caliburn from Mordred — her hand melts on contact, confirming the sword recognises its rightful wielder. The party uses debilitating spells: Darian's Goblin Pox Bomb (Sickened 2, Slowed 1) and Heart's Desire (Stupefied 2), while Bigby grapples her.

Morana casts a devastating **Harm spell** — Mordred dies taking the hit meant for Bigby (100 damage, instant death). During death, Mordred sees a chapel with raven-adorned shields and black-armoured followers addressing him as "Lord." An entity claims: "We've got a deal." Morana flees through a planar rift, bloodied but undefeated.

**Mother Gothel** leads an 8-hour resurrection ritual using Mordred's severed hand and gauntlet as a magical focus. Mordred returns with lasting penalties (Clumsy 1, Drained 1, Feebied 1 for one week). Darian's Medicine check (26) reveals Morana is a **construct** — bones held together by something other than natural sinew. Multiple characters have unexplained memory gaps: Morana doesn't remember before her 30s, Mother Gothel has 50-year gaps.

An Arcadian presence in the woods tells Darian: "People turned this latest has turned me sour... I will return to the land I came from, Arcadia."

- **Mother Gothel** — Leads resurrection ritual. Has 50-year memory gaps. Cannot harm living creatures. Warns against "the Lady Beyond the Mirror."

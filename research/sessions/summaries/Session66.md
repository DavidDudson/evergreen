# Session 66 Summary

**Date:** ~Mar 2026 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 66](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iRLTw6FzfqQ)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

Continuation of the circus battle from Session 65. Briar confirmed dead. Fight escalates — Ring Master and clown combatants coordinate attacks. Party uses dragon, spells, and tactical positioning. **Briar agrees to the Fool's deal** and returns from death — she must sleep in **5 dragon lairs**; she vomits blacksmith's tongs (random element). **Anastasia Tremaine's memories are restored** when **Captain Sharmaine** is killed by Drizella and Briar — he held her memories in an object. **Lady Tremaine confirmed alive** by the Fool/Polko: "a club over the head would kill her." **Rodrigo** (cursed lion from Cindershire) is freed by the party.

- **Captain Sharmaine** — Veiled witch's champion. Held Anastasia's memories in an object. Killed by Drizella and Briar.
- **Lady Tremaine** — Confirmed alive despite reports of death. "A club over the head would kill her."

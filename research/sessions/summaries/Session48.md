# Session 48 Summary

**Date:** ~Oct 2025 (Arc 7)
**YouTube:** [Session 48](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccGgqU_p2z0)
**Arc:** Arc 7 — Rymes Return / Greenwoods / Grimordia

## Synopsis

Party enters **Grimordia** — once a "shining beacon" city, now frozen ruins with citizens petrified in stone. A **black dragon** emerges from Grimordia's crater. The party discovers **Merlin's vault** beneath the old Imperial Palace and recovers four items: **Dragon Egg**, **Bracers of the Elements**, **Ring of Peerless Apprehension** (the same ring traded to Mary — recovered here), and the **Spectral Key**. **Maleficent** sleeps in the lower regions; the party avoids waking her.

## Key Events

- Grimordia entered: frozen ruins, petrified citizens, black dragon
- Merlin's vault beneath Imperial Palace; four items recovered
- Maleficent sleeps in the lower palace; party stealth-avoids her
- Black dragon and Maleficent connected (both lime-green eyes)

## Lore Reveals

- **Grimordia** — Sixth Age city, once magnificent, now ruins with frozen/petrified citizens.
- **Merlin's Vault** — Sealed beneath the Imperial Palace. Contains Dragon Egg, Bracers of Elements, Ring of Peerless Apprehension, Spectral Key.

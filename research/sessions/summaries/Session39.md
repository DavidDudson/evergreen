# Session 39 Summary

**Date:** ~Jun 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 39](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NEYgvjn1DUc)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

The party investigates a dock strike at Pebblecreek. **Spade Shipping Company** bought the Diamond Drover (Proud Mary) two months prior, fired the experienced dwarven crew, and brought in incompetent outsiders. The party meets **Captain Bitter Shaper** (dwarven female), pays her tab (55 silver), and learns something suspicious is happening beyond just corporate greed.

Darian infiltrates Spade Shipping HQ and finds the office of **Ida Montague** (mid-40s, High Counsellor representative under **Lady Pikes**). The office is suspiciously sterile — mattress never opened, no personal artifacts. In a drawer: a **glowing green rune-carved stone tablet with a deer skull design** — a control device that allows total puppet control (seeing through victim's eyes, controlling every muscle). This isn't witch magic; stone magic means it's ancient, purposeful, and permanent. Drizella recognises partial rune sets from her mother's work.

A mysterious elf at a bar has **glowing green eyes** (matching the elf Bigby spotted through scrying) and sends Drizella a paralytic poison from fool's lily. The elf has a vested interest in keeping the boat under current ownership.

## Key Events

- Dock strike investigation: Spade Shipping took over Diamond Drover, fired dwarven crew
- Captain Bitter Shaper met; crew suspects something beyond greed
- Ida Montague's sterile office found at Spade Shipping HQ
- Glowing green rune-carved stone tablet with deer skull — ancient control device
- Green-eyed elf poisons Drizella with paralytic from fool's lily
- Control tablet matches partial runes from Drizella's mother's work

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Captain Bitter Shaper** — Dwarven female captain. Experienced. Crew wants the ship back running properly.
- **Ida Montague** — Spade Shipping operative. Office suspiciously bare. Works under Lady Pikes (High Council).

## Lore Reveals

- **Control Magic Tablet** — Green rune-carved stone with deer skull. Allows total puppet control. Ancient/divine origin, not witch magic. Designed to last forever. Connected to Horned King imagery.
- **Spade Shipping** — Recent player with rapid expansion. Front for control-magic conspiracy under Lady Pikes.

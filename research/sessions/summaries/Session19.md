# Session 19 Summary

**Date:** ~Oct 2024 (Arc 4)
**YouTube:** [Session 19](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b4JVEuFwArw)
**Arc:** Arc 4 — Sea / Jolly Roger / Goback

## Synopsis

The session opens with a **Mordred knighting ceremony flashback**. Young Mordred was training at a Camelot academy under **Galehaut** (half-giant, one of the original Knights of the Round Table). During the final test — 16 candidates must best 3 opponents honourably — Mordred fights with supernatural speed, using trips rather than violence. As Galahad is about to be knighted, the **Beast of Camelot** attacks: an enormous creature of thorns, vines, and glowing green eyes with armour-corroding acid breath. Knights fall. Galahad is separated from Mordred. Galehaut carries Mordred to safety, saying: "You can't save everyone." Mordred never saw Galahad again.

In the present, the party investigates the **murder of Billy Bones** aboard the Hispaniola. Billy was found stabbed in a smuggler's compartment behind the kitchen during the pirate attack, killed by a **Camelot military dagger** (old, 5+ years, dragon stamp). Key evidence: a cigarette burn behind his ear, two sets of scuff marks in the compartment, and a dagger sheath found in the medical library. Billy had half of a treasure map that Captain Smallet wanted.

The investigation examines multiple suspects: **Henry Teig** (jailer, transporting the elf prisoner for 200 gold, hired by a short man — likely Memphis), **Squire Trone** (rich investor, missing a Camelot dagger from his collection), **Mr. Arrow** (First Mate who arranged the treasure-map speculation), and the **elf prisoner** itself (who may be magically influencing Henry, who is losing time/memory). The party establishes a curfew with soap and caltrops to catch nighttime movement.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Galehaut** (flashback) — Half-giant knight, one of the original Knights of the Round Table. Mordred's trainer at the Camelot academy. Carried Mordred to safety during the Beast attack. Said: "You can't save everyone."
- **Lieutenant Janet Nightingale** — Battle Mage, elf warrior aboard the Hispaniola.
- **Joe Anderson** — Bosun of the Hispaniola.
- **Henry Teig** — Jailer hired by the Jail Society to transport the elf prisoner to Misglas. Paid 200 gold by a short man (Memphis?). Losing time/memory near the prisoner.
- **Abraham Gray** — Carpenter's apprentice aboard the Hispaniola.
- **The Elf Prisoner** — Vine-covered entity chanting "As Above So Below." Missing one leg. Found near an altar surrounded by dead bodies and briars. Magical aura causes pain to detect. May be magically controlling Henry Teig.

## Notable Quotes

- **Galehaut** (to Mordred during Beast attack): "You can't save everyone."
- **Henry Teig** (under interrogation): "I've been losing time in the cage there."
- **Mr. Arrow** (addressing the crew): "Whoever has done this, if you are found and we dock you will have to face the Justice we find there. If you turn yourself in now, you will instead face my Justice which you may find to be slightly more forgiving."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question with alignment choices. Mordred's flashback implicitly poses the question of heroism vs. pragmatism — can you save everyone, or must you accept loss?

## Faction Developments

- **Jail Society** — Organisation that employs civilian jailers like Henry Teig. Contracted to transport the elf prisoner to Misglas. Uses crossed-keys symbol.
- **Camelot Military** — The murder weapon is a Camelot-issue dagger. Billy Bones had military connections. Knights once highly selective (16 per season), then expanded due to war, then collapsed.
- **Memphis Cadwallader** — Implied to be the "short man" who hired Henry Teig for 200 gold. His fingerprints are on the elf prisoner's transport.

## Quest Progress

- **Billy Bones Murder** — Active investigation. Suspects include Henry Teig, Squire Trone, Mr. Arrow. Evidence suggests someone with officer access and Camelot connections.
- **The Elf Prisoner** — Being transported to Misglas. Chants "As Above So Below." Magical influence on Henry Teig. May be an antiseed host.
- **The Treasure Map** — Two halves: one from Captain Smallet, one from Billy Bones (now deceased). Points to an island in the far south.
- **Galahad** — Mordred's best friend, separated during the Beast of Camelot attack. Fate unknown.

## Lore Reveals

- **The Beast of Camelot** — Creature of thorns, vines, glowing green eyes, toxic acid breath. Attacked during Mordred's knighting ceremony ~2-3 years ago. Destroyed the castle training grounds. Killed multiple knight candidates. Likely connected to Maleficent.
- **Galehaut** — One of the original Knights of the Round Table. Half-giant. Mordred's trainer.
- **Davey Jones' Bargain** — Maritime mythology: dead sailors at sea go to Davey Jones. He holds an auction; whoever has the most coins placed on them is brought back to life. Bad luck to die at sea without coins.
- **Camelot Military Daggers** — Standard issue to soldiers/knights. Dragon stamp. The murder weapon is at least 5 years old.
- **The Elf Prisoner's Origin** — Found at an altar near the University area (Ring's border), surrounded by dead bodies and thick briars. Continuously chanting. Missing one leg. Being transported to Misglas by the Jail Society.

# Session 12 Summary

**Date:** ~Mar 2024 (Arc 2)
**YouTube:** [Session 12](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zw8FNDYBzj8)
**Arc:** Arc 2 — The Winter Express / Rymes

## Synopsis

The party wakes to find themselves captured by witches of the Thrice-Triple Covens. **Hazel the Wise**, a 200+ year-old Grandmother, presides over a formal inquisition demanding information about Nimue's whereabouts and activities. Bigby sees through her intimidation (Society check 39) — she's actually scared. The party reveals what happened at the Observatory: Nimue's divination ritual, the destroyed machine, the stolen mask, and the recovered scabbard of Caliburn. The witches confirm Nimue has been a priority for their order, and this is the first confirmed sighting in years.

**Dean Marikosa** sends a shared vision to Bigby and Mordred showing the Fall of Arcadia — the great coalition assault on the Horned King's island, culminating in Merlin using the Mirror of Aretas to trap/destroy the King, after which Arcadia sank into the sea. Savvan, imprisoned in the city dungeons, reveals his reading: the Sunderance will occur within 6-18 months.

Memphis Cadwallader reappears, now posing as a wealthy gnome in a first-class carriage, and formally contracts the party to retrieve three items from Salasarglumm: the Ring of Objectification, the Rosewood Staff (of Beguiling), and the Looking Glass — for 5,000 gold. The party joins a caravan heading north toward Arona.

On the caravan, Drizella — now maskless after Nimue's theft — faces prejudice from the carriage usher but negotiates first-class passage. She encounters **The Creature** (Frankenstein's monster), who reveals that **Lady Tremaine** hired him to kill Anastasia. Drizella and the Creature form an uneasy alliance. Meanwhile, Bigby plays cards with an eccentric wealthy man called **Quint** — who, in the session's final twist, reveals himself as Bigby's brother, the **Big Bad Wolf**, beginning an assassination attempt.

## Key Events

- The Thrice-Triple Covens formally interrogate the party about Nimue; Hazel the Wise presides
- Dean Marikosa sends a vision of the Fall of Arcadia to Bigby and Mordred
- Savvan predicts the Sunderance within 6-18 months
- Memphis Cadwallader contracts the party to retrieve three items from Salasarglumm (5,000 gold reward)
- The witches provide a magical iron token (triquetra knot) — a blood-activated summoning device to contact them if Nimue is seen again
- The Creature reveals Lady Tremaine hired him to assassinate Anastasia; Drizella and the Creature form an alliance
- Bigby's brother (the Big Bad Wolf) is disguised as "Quint" on the caravan; reveals himself at session's end
- Drizella faces prejudice for her disfigured, maskless appearance but negotiates passage

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Hazel the Wise** — Grandmother witch (~200+ years old). Conducts the formal inquisition. Intimidating facade masking genuine fear about Nimue.
- **Agnes Crooked Finger** — Mother-rank witch, local representative. More diplomatic than Hazel; votes against violent tactics.
- **The Creature** (Frankenstein's monster) — Hideously scarred, stitched together. Hunting Victor Frankenstein. Reveals Lady Tremaine hired him to kill Anastasia. Forms alliance with Drizella.
- **Quint** (the Big Bad Wolf in disguise) — Eccentric bald man in teal doublet. Plays cards with Bigby using a mysterious tarot-like deck including a "Death" card. Revealed as Bigby's brother at the cliffhanger.
- **The Duchess of Bron** — Wealthy caravan passenger; seeks a cruise in her late husband's tradition. Minor comic relief.
- **Memphis Cadwallader** — Reappears disguised as a wealthy gnome; formalises the Salasarglumm contract.

## Notable Quotes

## Hero Question

No explicit hero question with alignment choices was identified in this session. The witches' interrogation served as an implicit moral test but without formal branching options.

## Faction Developments

- **Thrice-Triple Covens** — Actively hunting Nimue. Provide the party a magical summoning token. Internal disagreement about tactics (Hazel's intimidation vs. Agnes's diplomacy).
- **Memphis Cadwallader / Cities** — Formally contracts the party for the Salasarglumm mission. The three items (Ring, Staff, Looking Glass) are his primary interest.
- **Lady Tremaine** — Confirmed to have hired the Creature to assassinate Anastasia. Operating through intermediaries.
- **The Big Bad Wolf** — Infiltrated the caravan in disguise to get close to Bigby. Assassination attempt initiated.

## Quest Progress

- **Salasarglumm Contract** — Memphis formally hires the party. Targets: Ring of Objectification, Rosewood Staff of Beguiling, Looking Glass. Reward: 5,000 gold.
- **Find Anastasia / Protect Her** — The Creature reveals Lady Tremaine wants Anastasia dead. Drizella now has a direct lead.
- **Nimue's Location** — Witches provide summoning token for future sightings.
- **The Sunderance** — Savvan's prediction narrows the window to 6-18 months.

## Lore Reveals

- **The Fall of Arcadia** (Dean Marikosa's vision) — Arcadia was an island dominated by an enormous lime-green tree. The Horned King dwelt there amid nightmare creatures. A coalition of Titania, Mab, Merlin, the Gold Dragon, nine Grandmother Hags, and druids assaulted it. Merlin used the Mirror of Aretas to trap/destroy the Horned King. Arcadia sank into the sea.
- **Changelings** — Fey creatures who steal humanoids and replace them, taking their essence, appearance, and memories. Mordred's changeling nature resulted from his mother's deal with fey.
- **Coven Ranks** — Sisters (majority), Mothers (middle rank, like Agnes), Grandmothers (few, extremely powerful like Hazel).
- **Sibylla's exile** — She was exiled from the Covens for forbidden magic but is still covered under the "Haggish Concordant" (law 15).

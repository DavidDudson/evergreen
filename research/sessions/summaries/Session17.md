# Session 17 Summary

**Date:** ~Aug 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jfKxc0Zu1Nc)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia

## Synopsis

The session opens with a **Merida flashback**: as a teenager in the Frigid North, she attends a mysterious meeting at her family's hall where a tall veiled woman and a short figure in a brown coat speak with her parents. Her father takes her on a rite of passage into the Dark Woods to "meet the ancestors." At a stone plateau illuminated by otherworldly light, Merida encounters an **enormous bear** — described as a living mountain with weapons embedded in its skull. Her father fights the bear and loses his leg (gaining his peg leg), but Merida survives by meeting the bear's eyes with calm.

In the present, the party resolves the Creature confrontation at Victor Frankenstein's house. After multiple de-escalation attempts — Sibylla's Charm Person (fails), a guard's natural 20 Demoralise, Merida's critical success Intimidation — the Creature is calmed enough to descend to Victor's basement laboratory. There they find Tesla coils, multiple dead creature prototypes, and Victor frantically stitching a new creation. Victor confesses he couldn't fix the original Creature's mind and tried to build one that could "reason with" it. The Creature (given the name **Adam**) and Victor have an emotional reconciliation.

Meanwhile, in a parallel thread, **Bigby** accompanies **Titus** (Titus Skye — an old orc ranger) to the Guard House where Jacob Hilton is imprisoned. Titus interrogates and then kills Hilton. When Bigby confronts him, Titus produces a mirror artifact; Bigby shatters it, creating a portal vortex that hurls both through multiple locations. They crash down a chimney, covered in glass lacerations. Time desynchronisation: the party experienced ~12 hours, but Bigby and Titus experienced 2-3 months subjectively.

The party discovers they have unknowingly signed a **devil's contract** — Memphis Cadwallader is the intermediary for Rumpelstiltskin/Lucifer. Coded letters found in Victor's lab spell out "Rumpelstiltskin" when read backwards. Prior parties were cycled through similar contracts. Victor also signed one. The contract includes bizarre clauses (no more than 3 glasses of milk per day, non-silver weapons only).

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Titus Skye** — Old orc ranger. Scarred face, salt-and-pepper moustache, green cape, massive war axe. Can summon a griffin-like creature. Killed Jacob Hilton during interrogation. Bound by a devil contract. Involved in mirror-portal accident with Bigby.
- **Adam** (The Creature) — Given his name by Victor during reconciliation. Sought companionship and understanding, not revenge. Reconciles with his creator.
- **Victor Frankenstein** — Cowardly but capable surgeon. Created multiple creature prototypes (all failed except Adam). Also signed a devil's contract. Provides the party with elixirs and healing notes.
- **Elizabeth** — Victor's wife. Kind-hearted. Has an infant child (William). Invited the party to dinner before the Creature's arrival.
- **Fergus** — Merida's father. Ancient bear hunter with a peg leg (lost fighting the Great Bear). Protective of Merida.
- **The Great Bear** — Enormous, possibly magical creature in the Dark Woods. "Living mountain" with weapons embedded in its skull. Part of a northern rite of passage.

## Lore Reveals

- **Devil's Contracts** — Memphis Cadwallader operates multi-generational contract cycles. Prior parties have been "cycled out." Contract clauses include: non-silver weapons, max 3 glasses of milk/day, no magical items in war.
- **The Great Bear** — Northern Dark Woods creature. Enormous, ancient, possibly divine. Weapons embedded in its skull. Part of a rite of passage. Understood human intent (calm vs. fear).
- **Mirror-Portal Transportation** — Shattering mirror artifacts creates unstable portals. Causes severe time desynchronisation (12 hours real time = 2-3 months subjective). Glass embeds in travellers.
- **Frankenstein's Laboratory** — Tesla coils, multiple dead creature prototypes, mechanical constructors. Victor attempted to create a companion creature that could "reason" with Adam.
- **Merida's Family** — Father Fergus lost his leg fighting the Great Bear. Mother associated with mysterious veiled visitors. Northern rite of passage involves confronting the bear.

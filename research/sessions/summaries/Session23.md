# Session 23 Summary

**Date:** ~Nov 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 23](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ACHeCfKW5cg)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The party enters Salasarglumm and is given a tour by **Tacho**, a polite city guide. The city has curfew enforcement, reports of missing children and cats, and predators in the sewers. Bigby is offered a sewer monster-hunting contract, but first discovers a **disguised assassin** running a barber shop — the barber attempts to slit Bigby's throat via a trapdoor trap. Bigby defeats him and finds a hidden basement: a horrific industrial setup with a boiler, furnace, meat hooks, and bloodstained floor drains, plus sewer access.

The party navigates flooded sewer sections in boats, finding evidence of a previous civilisation (submerged furniture, old pulley systems). They encounter six **Cobalt Scouts** — blind reptilian creatures with leather cone headpieces (echolocation-based perception), eyes completely removed, serving "the Great One." A prolonged boat-based combat ensues.

The final cobalt fires an arrow coated in **basilisk poison**. Mordred interposes himself to protect the party and is **turned to stone** — mechanically dead but reversible. The boat capsizes. As the party struggles to retrieve petrified Mordred from underwater, a **mysterious woman with pink eyes** uses supernatural shadow-force to pull Mordred from the water, declaring: "I need him still." All lights extinguish during the rescue.

The party applies treated basilisk blood to reverse the petrification. Mordred wakes screaming from a trauma vision — his childhood home, a confrontation with his mother in dark raven form saying: "We will be meeting again... You'll be coming home." Mordred mechanically dies from accumulated basilisk poison, but Drizella accepts power from **Morgana's raven** and revives him with enhanced healing.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Tacho** — Polite city guide in Salasarglumm. Briefs party on curfew, missing children/cats, and sewer monsters.
- **The Barber/Assassin** — Disguised murderer running a barber shop. Identifies Bigby and attempts assassination. Has an industrial victim-processing basement.
- **Cobalt Scouts** (x6) — Blind reptilian creatures with leather cone headpieces (sound-based perception). Eyes completely removed. Serve "the Great One." Immune to disease. Speak Common/Draconic. Use basilisk-poison arrows.
- **Mysterious woman with pink eyes** — Unseen figure who rescues petrified Mordred from underwater using shadow-force. Declares: "I need him still." Likely Morgana Le Fay.
- **Morgana's Raven** — Dark familiar. Offers power to Drizella. Kills Darian's dog as moral lesson. Acts as intermediary for Morgana.

## Notable Quotes

- **Mordred's mother** (in trauma vision): "We will be meeting again... You'll be coming home."
- **Darian** (to Mordred): "Life is not given to all of us equally. Do not throw yours away... care about your own life."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question. The session's central moral tension: Drizella's pacifism vs. the necessity of violence for survival, and whether accepting Morgana's power is worth the moral cost.

## Faction Developments

- **"The Great One"** — Unknown entity commanding the Cobalt Scouts. Removes their eyes, forces sound-based perception, uses basilisk poison. Commands loyalty unto death.
- **Morgana Le Fay** — Intervening through her raven familiar. Offers power with moral strings attached. The pink-eyed woman rescuing Mordred is likely her.
- **Salasarglumm Underworld** — Assassin networks operating within the city. The barber's victim-processing basement suggests organised crime or worse.

## Quest Progress

- **Salasarglumm Infiltration** — Party has entered the city. Sewer exploration reveals deeper threats.
- **Missing Children/Cats** — Likely connected to sewer creatures (Cobalt Scouts or "the Great One").
- **The Three Items** — Not yet located. Party still establishing themselves in the city.
- **Mordred's Survival** — Revived but traumatised. Morgana's interest in him confirmed.

## Lore Reveals

- **Cobalt Scouts** — Blind reptilian creatures modified/cursed by "the Great One." Eyes completely removed. Sound-based echolocation via leather cone headpieces. Immune to disease. Serve loyally unto death.
- **Basilisk Biology** — Blood can be treated/preserved as "basilisk oil." Petrification is reversible with proper application. Cobalts are immune to basilisk disease despite handling it.
- **Salasarglumm's Sewers** — Pre-engineered system with old pulley mechanisms, drainage channels. Contains remnants of a previous civilisation (submerged furniture, tools).
- **Morgana's Power** — Can be channelled through her raven familiar to enhance healing spells. Comes with moral obligations/tests.

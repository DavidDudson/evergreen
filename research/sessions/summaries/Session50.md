# Session 50 Summary

**Date:** ~Oct 2025 (Arc 7)
**YouTube:** [Session 50](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MfS9l4YSWe4)

## Synopsis

Final vault raid on Grimordia Imperial. Party defeats Memphis (Rumpelstiltskin), destroys his contracts, reveals his flaming true form. Royal court and Chanels perish in vault collapse. Treasures acquired: **Mask of Mutable Clay** (Briar — shapeshifting), **Hand of Sycorax** (Drizella — cursed witch's hand), **History Book of Druid Circles** (Bigby), **Golden Scarab** (two-piece necklace), and **Crimson Dragon Cloak** (Mordred — cursed, transformative).

## Key Events

- Memphis defeated; contracts destroyed; true flaming form revealed
- Royal court Grimordia made final stand in vault against Maleficent
- Multiple cursed/powerful artifacts acquired
- Daisy Chanel reconciles with party (diplomacy 38)

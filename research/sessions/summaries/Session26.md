# Session 26 Summary

**Date:** ~Dec 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 26](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q8xDIC02Zzc)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The party travels to **Mimsy**, a farming town ruled by **Baron Drazag** (a brutal lumber merchant installed by the Beast King). The town is oddly new — most buildings constructed within 4-5 years. They discover a horrifying truth beneath the church: **Mother Ren** is performing healing miracles powered by an **infernal fey demon** chained in a demi-plane beneath the building. The old priest Father Dorth had bound the demon daily, but when he died, Mother Ren took over the binding, channelling its power for healing while the demon feeds on the town's suffering.

The party descends into the demi-plane — twisted architecture with red coral growth, warped flesh creatures, and the demon itself: thin, emaciated, red-skinned, huge teeth, wrapped in chains. Drizella uses a negotiation spell to parley with the demon. When she asks it to target "Lady Tremaine," the demon's confused response suggests Lady Tremaine may exist in multiple forms or is somehow not singular. The bargain fails and the demi-plane begins collapsing — walls crumble into infinite void. The party escapes; Mother Ren emerges with an ancient chained book (**The Sanctus Codex**).

A divine voice blesses the party: 20 temporary HP, Resistance 5 to fire/evil/void/spirit damage, and +1 to persuasion. Mother Ren is freed from her demonic obligation.

Through skilled diplomacy (Bigby's critical roll against Drazag's intimidation), the party convinces **Baron Drazag** to join their coup against the Beast King. First baron secured.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Baron Drazag** — Brutal woodcutter baron. Blood-thirsty but pragmatic. Successfully recruited to the coup alliance through diplomacy.
- **Mother Ren** — Cleric, identical twin to Atasha. Channelled an infernal fey's power for healing. Freed from demonic pact. Granted party The Sanctus Codex.
- **The Infernal Fey/Demon** — Red-skinned, emaciated creature chained in a demi-plane beneath the church. Feeds on misery and suffering. Hints that Lady Tremaine is not singular.
- **Galen Aran** — Tristan's uncle. Hidden rebel leader. Reveals Borograth Tower and the Beast King's enchanted staff.
- **Governor Moro** — Revealed as the old Lord's brother. Confirms coup requires two of three barons.

## Hero Question

Implicit moral dilemma: can a creature bound to cause misery be negotiated with, or is freeing it an acceptable cost? The party chose grace and negotiation over violence.

## Lore Reveals

- **Infernal Fey** — Creatures of misery, untimely death, and random tragedy. "Flutter around battlefields" feeding on suffering. Can be bound by daily rituals.
- **Beast King's Staff** — Located in Borograth Tower. Projects mind-control enchantment across all of Salasarglumm, keeping the population compliant.
- **Lady Tremaine's Nature** — The demon's confused reaction when asked about her suggests she is not a single entity — possibly multiple forms, a transformation, or an illusion.
- **Mimsy** — Farming town, only 4-5 years old. No defensive walls. Dangerous manual labour. Citizens rely on Mother Ren's healing.

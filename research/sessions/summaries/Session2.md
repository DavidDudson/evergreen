# Session 2 Summary

**Date:** Sep 2023
**YouTube:** [Session 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mPq_jE13bDM)
**Arc:** Arc 1 — Lowmarsh (Sessions 1–6)

## Synopsis

The party departs Lowmarsh through the eastern gate heading northwest into the Greenwoods toward the hilltop heath — the battlefield where Camelot soldiers were massacred. Bigby shifts into his hybrid wolf form outside town, alarming a guard. Sibylla and Merida lead the way, being familiar with the forest.

En route they encounter an unnamed dwarf traveler who speaks only Dwarvish. He warns them via chalk drawings: the Crest of Camelot (a rearing dragon) connected to a deer skull with antlers, miming destruction while saying the word "Bashaba." As they approach the battlefield, an unnatural darkness falls — pitch black at 2:30 PM, identified as very old primordial magic.

At the battlefield they find dead Camelot soldiers with organs surgically removed. A tall cloaked figure wearing a deer skull mask with glowing green eyes — the Deer-Masked Stranger — is harvesting hearts from the dead with surgical tools. It communicates telepathically: "This one is already dead" and "I am done with this place. Have what you will." Then it flies away with green will-o-wisp lights orbiting it.

The party fights seven ghouls in a chaotic battle. Bigby is bitten and fails a fortitude save (ghoul fever incubation). Nik Jones involuntarily manifests fire magic through his sword (Spellstrike with Scorching Blast) and shadow-teleportation (Dimensional Assault), then blacks out with no memory of it — terrified to discover he has magic. The party subdues one ghoul alive (Bigby carries it on his shoulder), destroys the rest, and burns the corpses on a pyre to prevent regeneration. The localized darkness lifts as they leave the area. They recover a Camelot insignia from a dead soldier.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

### New NPCs
- **The Deer-Masked Stranger** — Tall cloaked figure with a deer skull mask and glowing green eyes. Communicates telepathically. Harvests hearts from dead soldiers with surgical tools. Green will-o-wisp lights orbit it. Identified as a powerful occult spellcaster engaged in cult activity, far above the party's level. Not a hag or witch. (Later revealed to be Nimue.)
- **Unnamed Dwarf Traveler** — Short, squat, pale skin, close-cropped beard, buzz-shaved head, hiking stick, large pack. Speaks only Dwarvish. Drew the Crest of Camelot and a deer skull, said "Bashaba," warned the party about the Stranger.

### New PC Details
- **Bigby** — Dragon Instinct Barbarian. Jaw/bite natural attacks (d10+6). Bitten by ghoul, failed fortitude save (ghoul fever).
- **Nik Jones** — Magus class. Spellstrike + Arcane Cascade. Fire magic and shadow-teleportation manifest involuntarily. Speaks Infernal unconsciously. Enters a trance combat state then blacks out. Rubs two spots on his forehead (horns growing?). Father would disapprove of magic.
- **Mordred** — Champion class. Glimpse of Redemption reaction (forces enemy to choose: deal no damage or become enfeebled 2). Sword has an Iron Mark handle (from Iron-skin trees), briar/feather hilt pattern, and a "W" inscription on the pommel he tried to scratch off. Demoralizes via guilt-tripping.
- **Sibylla** — Mentor is **Bessa of the Lone Pine**. Has a familiar named **Sean** (black cat). Grew up in the Greenwoods. Speaks Common and Sylvan. Previously a hag in a past life.

## Lore Reveals

- **The Parallax** — An upcoming astronomical event (winter solstice + full moon, 2–3 days away). During this event: prophecy is at maximum power ("much more accurate, clear, and precise"), occult/cult spellcasters become significantly stronger. The Deer-Masked Stranger appears to be timing a ritual to coincide with it.
- **Primordial magic** — The localized supernatural darkness at the battlefield is identified as "very old, primordial" magic — ancient and rare. It lifted when the party left the area.
- **Iron Mark** — A material harvested from Iron-skin trees deep in the Greenwoods. Harder and lighter than steel. Used in Mordred's sword handle/guard.
- **Ghoul regeneration** — Ghoul corpses reanimate within a couple of days unless burned, decapitated, or destroyed with radiant damage.
- **Crest of Camelot** — A dragon rearing up on two feet, wings at the back.
- **Nik Jones's latent powers** — Fire magic, shadow-teleportation, and unconscious Infernal speech suggest an infernal/tiefling heritage he is unaware of. Forehead rubbing may indicate horns growing.
- **"Bashaba"** — Word used by the dwarf traveler when describing the deer-skull figure. Significance unclear; may be a name or title.

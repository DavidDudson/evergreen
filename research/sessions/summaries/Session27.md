# Session 27 Summary

**Date:** ~Dec 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OQfl8NFK_z4)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The party hunts a giant boar in the Greenwoods as a gift to impress **Baron Drazag**. They discover ancient giant bones and ruins, and the boar locked in combat with a massive magical fish-creature. After defeating the fish-creature, the party allies with a surviving giant by offering gemstones and a gold skull with ruby eyes (which changes colour — blue to red — suggesting it contains a demonic soul).

At Baron Drazag's manor, the volatile Baron demands proof of strength: Mordred arm-wrestles him. Drazag wins the brutal contest, then **cuts off Mordred's left hand** with a carving knife. Moments later, **Morgana's murder-crow swarm** — thousands of magical crows — attacks the manor, killing Drazag and innocent townspeople. Morgana's crow delivers a **magical black gauntlet** (9th-rank modified spell construct, rune-inscribed) to Mordred as a replacement hand.

Mordred is emotionally devastated — guilt over the innocent deaths, loss of his hand, and Morgana's violent intervention. He attempts to leave the party permanently. Drizella comforts him with storytelling and acupuncture therapy. In a dream, Morgana sends a message expressing love; Mordred rejects her: "If you follow me again I will make sure you'll never be able to find me."

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Baron Drazag** — Volatile, eccentric baron. Won't use his left hand (injury/limitation). Demands arm-wrestling as proof of strength. Cuts off Mordred's hand. Killed by Morgana's crow swarm.
- **The Giant** — Ancient being in the Greenwoods among giant skeleton bones. Intelligent. Accepts alliance with the party in exchange for gemstones and the gold skull.

## Notable Quotes

- **Baron Drazag:** "A warrior's greatest strength is their greatest weakness."
- **Mordred** (to Morgana in dream): "If you follow me again I will make sure that you'll never be able to find me."
- **Drizella** (bedtime story to Mordred): "Story of the great hero who caught a spinning star and used it to chase the darkness away."

## Hero Question

No formal hero question. Central moral tension: Morgana's violent intervention killed innocents to protect/help Mordred — are the party complicit?

## Faction Developments

- **Morgana Le Fay** — Commands 9th-rank magic. Capable of mass destruction via crow swarm. Emotionally invested in Mordred. Acts independently and violently.
- **Baron Drazag** — Dead. Killed by Morgana's crows. His barony is now in chaos.
- **Beast King's Regime** — Propaganda statue in town softens his image. Regime increasingly fragile with baron assassinated.

## Quest Progress

- **Baron Recruitment** — Drazag was secured but then killed by Morgana. His death may still count for the coup. Second baron still needed.
- **Mordred's Hand** — Lost. Replaced by magical black gauntlet (9th-rank, rune-inscribed). Physical and psychological adjustment needed.
- **Giant Alliance** — Secured. Giant needs proof of service after "the ball."

## Lore Reveals

- **Magical Black Gauntlet** — 9th-rank modified spell construct. Rune-inscribed. Provides physical function replacement for Mordred's lost hand. Glows with runes when activated. Gift from Morgana via crow.
- **Gold Skull Artifact** — Contains a demonic soul or spirit. Ruby eyes change colour (blue to red) when given to the giant.
- **Ancient Giants** — Bones in the Greenwoods suggest a previous age of giants. At least one intelligent giant survives.
- **Greenwoods Hazards** — Forest distorts space/perception. Has "light" and "dark" zones. Inhabited by magical creatures.

# Session 14 Summary

**Date:** ~Jun 2024 (Arc 3)
**YouTube:** [Session 14](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2cnWELGPJDk)
**Arc:** Arc 3 — Oronia

## Synopsis

The party confronts **Dr. Jacob Hilton** in his office after he consumes a transformation potion, becoming a horrific elongated skeletal creature with protruding bone spines — the Devil of Newgate. Combat is intense; a new PC, **Darian Sand**, arrives mid-fight via a teleportation mishap and joins the party. The party subdues Hilton and Drizella heals him to prevent death so he can face justice.

Under truth serum (rosemary, vegetable oil, eelskin), Hilton reveals he discovered a mirror shard in a hidden desk compartment and another in the university catacombs bearing the symbol of the Order of the Eleventh Eye. Through these shards, **Bloody Mary** communicated with him, warning him about the witches and teaching him alchemical formulas. Hilton murdered Order Arcania members out of paranoid conviction that the witches were plotting something.

The party splits into three teams. Drizella and Mordred perform the mirror ritual from Hilton's journal — combining two mirror shards, blocking light, and speaking Mary's name three times. Both critically fail their saves, becoming severely frightened and doomed. **Bloody Mary** speaks through the mirror, offering Drizella a boon: a chunk of mirror that grants Locate Object (500 ft range), at the cost of 10 max HP and the permanent inability to experience joy. Drizella eats the mirror shard.

Meanwhile, Bigby reveals his true identity to the Secret Order Arcania, earning grudging respect from their leader **Titus**. At the Chantry, Merida meets **Moonam** (the local witch leader), who confirms the Red Hand blockade is specifically about retrieving an artifact the witches possess.

## Key Events

- Dr. Jacob Hilton transforms and is defeated in combat; subdued alive for trial
- Darian Sand joins the party mid-combat via teleportation accident
- Hilton reveals Bloody Mary communicated through mirror shards, teaching him formulas and manipulating him
- Drizella and Mordred summon Bloody Mary — both critically fail saves (Frightened 11-12, Doomed)
- Drizella eats a mirror shard from Mary, gaining Locate Object but losing 10 max HP and the ability to feel joy
- Drizella glimpses her mother's reflection during the ritual
- Bigby reveals his identity to the Order Arcania; earns Titus's respect
- Merida and Darian meet the Chantry leader Moonam; learn the blockade targets a specific artifact
- Lord Edmund pays the party 50 silver each and asks them to investigate the witches

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Darian Sand** — New PC. Investigator/fighter. Arrives via teleportation. Carries a parakeet familiar called Nightshade and a silvery coin from a mysterious employer.
- **Titus** — Leader of the Secret Order Arcania. Rides an owl. Green armour, pepper moustache. Older warrior who tests loyalty via mind-reading. Answers to a higher authority.
- **Moonam** — Leader of the Oronia Chantry (witch order). Diplomatic but firm. Reveals the Red Hand blockade targets a specific artifact.
- **Bloody Mary** — First direct contact. Speaks through joined mirror shards in a polite, motherly voice. Offers supernatural boons at terrible costs. Gave Hilton formulas and warnings.
- **Lord Edmund** — City leader. Pays the party and hires them to investigate the witches.

## Lore Reveals

- **Mirror of Aretas / Shard of Erastus** — Ancient artifact from the 3rd-6th age. Destroyed long ago into shards. Multiple pieces exist (Hilton's desk, university catacombs with OXIE mark). When pieces join, they glow yellow and extinguish nearby candles. Acts as focal point for Bloody Mary's presence.
- **Bloody Mary** — Trapped in the mirror. Female, polite, motherly voice. Can grant supernatural abilities at terrible costs. Was imprisoned (method unknown). Gave knowledge to Hilton.
- **Truth Serum** — Can be crafted from rosemary, vegetable oil, and eelskin.
- **Order Arcania** — Secret spellcaster order. Symbol: owl. Conducts 7th-level teleportation rituals. Answers to a higher mysterious authority.

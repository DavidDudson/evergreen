# Session 37 Summary

**Date:** ~May 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 37](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3sJBjdK-Ffs)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

Festival of the Rising Dawn continues. Bigby won the archery tournament; Mordred placed 2nd in the joust. Drizella reunites with her mentor **Thornwell Veil** — an experimental healer performing cutting-edge surgical techniques on tournament casualties. Darian encounters another elemental child (wind).

**Bigby** is cornered by **Flycatcher (Edward Basque)** — his old supervisor from the Hogson gang. Flycatcher and cronies beat Bigby and demand he break into Sunspire jail to kill **Greta the witch**, threatening death to the entire party if he refuses. Flycatcher is watching Bigby in the city.

Mordred's conversation with Lancelot continues: Lancelot confirms he will accompany Mordred to the university. Mordred has no interest in seeking Merlin. Mordred is confirmed as the missing heir to the Pendragon line.

The Krennic deal: initial offer 150gp, Memphis has standing orders to double any offer (300gp total). Midnight deadline. The party passes around the **Scrying Orb** and it activates — Drizella sees her **father emaciated and chained** in the vision space.

## Key Events

- Flycatcher (Hogson gang) blackmails Bigby: break into Sunspire jail and kill Greta, or the party dies
- Drizella reunites with mentor Thornwell Veil (experimental healer)
- Lancelot confirms he'll escort Mordred to the university
- Scrying Orb activates: Drizella sees her father emaciated and chained
- Krennic's Looking Glass offer: 150gp (Memphis doubles to 300gp). Midnight deadline.

# Session 38 Summary

**Date:** ~Jun 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 38](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZLWqE8LuXQ)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

The party breaks **Greta** out of Sunspire jail. She is revealed as the daughter of **Amir High** (dwarven city leader). Greta's magic connects to the moon (opposite to her sun-connected family), and has become unpredictable in the past year. She has 6 sisters; one (**Freda**) has been asleep for 8 months after Greta accidentally gave her bad dreams — she hasn't woken. In each generation, one sister is placed in a tower (systematic confinement). Another sister (**Arabella**) is to be married to Flycatcher, which would give him control of the city via inheritance.

**Irene Adler** (crimson cloak, scar across face) arrives and hires the party to stop Flycatcher's marriage to Arabella — or kill him. Her employer is implied to be **Moriarty**. She whispers to Bigby: "Excellent work on Xander. A shame you were too slow." Greta has been receiving coded letters from an unknown benefactor helping orchestrate her escape: "Be ready. I will find you. This is not a threat."

## Key Events

- Party breaks Greta out of Sunspire jail (Guard Captain killed during escape)
- Greta revealed as daughter of Amir High (dwarven city leader)
- Greta's moon magic is inverse to her family's sun magic; becoming unpredictable
- Sister Freda asleep 8 months (Greta's accidental curse); locked in tower
- Systematic family pattern: one daughter confined per generation
- Arabella to marry Flycatcher — would give Hogsons control of the city
- Irene Adler hired by Moriarty to stop the Flycatcher-Arabella marriage
- Greta receiving coded letters from unknown benefactor orchestrating her escape

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Greta** — Princess, daughter of Amir High. Moon magic (inverse to family's sun). 6 sisters. Accidentally cursed sister Freda to sleep. Heals party with moonlight magic.
- **Irene Adler** (Irene Adign) — Crimson red cloak. Scar through eyebrow. Agent of Moriarty. Hired to stop Flycatcher's marriage to Arabella. References Bigby's past work: "Excellent work on Xander."
- **Freda** — Greta's sister. Asleep 8 months in a tower from Greta's accidental curse.
- **Arabella** — Greta's sister. To marry Flycatcher. Marriage would transfer city control.

## Faction Developments

- **Hogsons / Flycatcher** — Marrying into dwarven royalty (Arabella) for city control.
- **Moriarty (via Irene Adler)** — Actively opposing Flycatcher's political rise.
- **Unknown Benefactor** — Orchestrating Greta's escape via coded letters. Uses magical transmutation cipher.

## Quest Progress

- **Stop Flycatcher's Marriage** — Irene Adler's contract. Alternative to killing him.
- **Wake Freda** — Greta's sister asleep 8 months from accidental curse.
- **Unknown Benefactor** — Who is writing coded letters to Greta?

## Lore Reveals

- **Greta's Family** — 7 daughters. Sun-connected magic (except Greta = moon). Systematic tower confinement: one daughter per generation.
- **Moon Magic** — Inverse of family's sun healing. Drains rather than heals when unstable. Becoming more extreme.
- **Irene Adler / Moriarty** — Operating in Pebblecreek. Irene references Bigby's past assassination work in Xander.

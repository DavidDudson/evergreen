# Session 7 Summary

**Date:** Dec 2023
**YouTube:** [Session 7](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-QBsTQihF7s)
**Arc:** Arc 2 — The Winter Express / Rymes (Sessions 7–12)

## Synopsis

The session opens with the party on the road heading toward Rymes after escaping Lowmarsh. Merida leads the march; Memphis Cadwallader and Savvan travel with them. Savvan explains the four streams of magic to Nik Jones (Arcane, Occult, Divine, Primal) and notes that Nik's spell-punching is unusual even among the magically gifted — Ersatz University would be the place to get answers.

The **Winter Express** arrives — a magical train that materialises on tracks that did not exist moments before. The skeleton **Conductor** greets the party by name; everyone finds a one-way ticket to Rymes already in their pockets, pre-punched with cryptic letters (Mordred: "M R"; Nik: "P C"; Sibylla: "R M I"; Merida: "F I"; Bigby: "L N R Y"; Drizella: "F T"). The train submerges into the Underdark through a drill-like mechanism, travelling at enormous speed through Dwarven cave systems, crystal caverns, and underground farms.

During the journey, **Memphis Cadwallader** reveals his commission: the party must travel to **Salasarglumm** and retrieve three items stolen by the **Beast King** — a Ring, a Looking Glass, and a third item — for his employer, **Queen Maeve of Avalon** ("the Silver Lady"). He offers 10,000 silver per item plus a letter of introduction to Ersatz University. Combat erupts when **peppermint constructs** from Gumdrop Canyon burst into the passenger carriage; Nik Jones goes down (dying 1) before the party defeats them. The Conductor rewards the party with magical gifts.

A second skeleton — **the Hitchhiker**, the Conductor's scruffier brother — boards the train from outside and returns the Conductor's dropped pocket watch. As the train surfaces, it brakes suddenly when thousands of reindeer block the tracks. The party encounters **the Winter Giver** — a jolly old man in a red cloak with a reindeer sleigh — who has crash-landed after buzzing soldiers bearing a Golden Lion banner. As he departs, a second demonic face briefly appears at the back of his head. The session closes with Savvan laying out tarot cards (the Hanged Man visible) as the train arrives at the outskirts of Rymes.

## Key Events

- **The Winter Express arrives**: magical train with skeleton Conductor; pre-punched tickets with cryptic letters for each PC
- **Four streams of magic explained**: Arcane, Occult, Divine, Primal — Nik's spell-punching is unusual even for innate casters
- **Memphis reveals the Salasarglumm commission**: retrieve three items (Ring, Looking Glass, third item) from the Beast King for Queen Maeve; 10,000 silver per item
- **Peppermint construct ambush**: living peppermints from Gumdrop Canyon attack the train; Nik goes down (dying 1) before the party wins
- **Magical gifts from the Conductor**: each PC receives a unique item as thanks
- **The Hitchhiker boards**: the Conductor's brother, a trickster skeleton who rides outside the train
- **The Winter Giver encounter**: Father Christmas analogue who is also (by lore) an evil spirit; demonic second face briefly visible
- **Golden Lion soldiers spotted**: unknown faction from the "Far East," not seen in the region for many years
- **Savvan's tarot cards**: seen laying out cards at session's end; the Hanged Man visible

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

### New NPCs
- **The Conductor** — Skeleton in a bright blue suit with white stripes, pocket watch, skull for a face. Operates the Winter Express. Gives magical gifts to passengers who defend the train. Polite and formal.
- **The Hitchhiker** — Scruffily dressed skeleton (ratty scarf, multiple coats, fingerless gloves, beanie). The Conductor's brother. Rides on the outside of the train. Trickster figure.
- **The Winter Giver** — Old man in a big red cloak with a long white beard, riding a reindeer sleigh (one has a glowing red nose). Jolly gift-giver but also, by lore, a very evil spirit that devours and punishes the wicked. A second demonic face briefly appears at the back of his head.
- **The Tea Lady** — Elderly woman pushing a cart on the train. Serves jasmine tea.

### Returning NPCs
- **Memphis Cadwallader** — Reveals his employer is Queen Maeve of Avalon; commissions the party to retrieve three items from Salasarglumm
- **Savvan** — Explains magic to Nik; seen laying out tarot cards (the Hanged Man) at session's end

### PC Details
- **Nik Jones** — Goes down (dying 1) in the peppermint fight; stabilised. His spell-punching is flagged as anomalous even among innate casters.
- **Sibylla** — Her mother used tarot cards (connection to Savvan's cards noted)

## Hero Question

No formal hero question this session. The DM used a meta hero-point card exercise at the session open ("what is your character's fatal flaw?") — answers included overconfidence and "my past" (Sibylla).

## Faction Developments

- **Queen Maeve / Avalon** — Memphis confirms he works for Queen Maeve. She wants three stolen magic items recovered from the Beast King of Salasarglumm. She has been at war with Camelot for ~20 years and killed the Dwarven Barons at the war's start.
- **The Beast King of Salasarglumm** — First named and described. A rogue alteration wizard (ex-Ersatz University student) who used a botched polymorph to become a monster, conquered Salasarglumm ~4 years ago with constructs. Has immense physical power, flight, and darkvision.
- **Golden Lion (unknown faction)** — Soldiers bearing a Golden Lion banner encountered offscreen, being harassed by the Winter Giver. From the "Far East," not seen in the region for many years.

## Lore Reveals

- **The Underdark ("the Under")** — Vast cave system beneath the Greenwoods. Fey rules still apply near some water sources. Races: Dwarves, Deep Elves, Stone-skin Goblins, Blinders, Shades. Major cities accessible via great elevators.
- **Four streams of magic** — Arcane, Occult ("the Arult"), Divine, Primal. Most people are gifted with one; those accessing multiple are rare.
- **Salasarglumm** — A Glade in Gribsby. Four years ago, a rogue alteration wizard used polymorph magic on himself, became a monster, conquered it with constructs, and now rules as the Beast King.
- **Queen Maeve / Avalon** — Avalon is a kingdom to the northeast; Maeve has been at war with Camelot for ~20 years. She killed the Dwarven Barons at the war's start. Described as a powerful sorceress and collector of magical items.
- **The Winter Giver** — Dual nature: jolly gift-giver and evil spirit that devours the wicked. Fey-adjacent but not strictly Fey.
- **Peppermint Constructs** — Naturally animated creatures from Gumdrop Canyon in the mines; periodic train hazards. Weak to fire; low will saves.
- **Ticket Letters** — Each PC's punched ticket contains incomplete letters building toward words or names — an ongoing mystery/foreshadowing mechanic.

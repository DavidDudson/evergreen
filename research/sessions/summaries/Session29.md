# Session 29 Summary

**Date:** ~Jan 2025 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 29](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rcmGCtVLyhc)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

A shorter, transitional session focused on ball preparation. Mordred seeks dance lessons from Drizella. The party picks up custom ball outfits from the tailor **Franço**: Drizella in a polka-dot dress, Mordred with a gold raven mask, Bigby in a black-and-gold suit with wolf head, Darian in cobalt blue with a mask.

En route to the ball venue, the party encounters **Pathkeeper Ro** (an orcish woman with a militia-like group of four armed companions). Ro is tracking the giant the party previously encountered — she believes it's being controlled by someone using a "small blade of black" and shares a scar/pact with the creature. She asks the party to help investigate the giant's controller after the ball. The party bluffs about their involvement (deception check 27).

The ball invitation map contains hidden illusions — Roman numerals reveal concealed locations when the illusions are broken, including a Witch's Tower and auction house.

# Session 24 Summary

**Date:** ~Nov 2024 (Arc 5)
**YouTube:** [Session 24](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fKe2WFyk4_0)
**Arc:** Arc 5 — Salasarglumm

## Synopsis

The party defeats the **Basilisk** in its lair — an old alchemical workshop in the Salasarglumm sewers. Drizella nearly dies from a Basilisk bite (Dying 2) but is saved by Mordred's healing. They discover multiple petrified victims (guards, Woodland Watchers, labourers, townsfolk) and collect the Basilisk eye as bounty proof (500 silver reward). **Kristoff Marlo** reveals he raised the Basilisk for alchemical research — basilisk blood has restorative properties and can reverse petrification when properly fermented (1-3 months). The oldest bottle was used on Mordred and worked.

The party is summoned to the Beast King's castle. **Atasha** acts as primary liaison. **Lamere**, the Beast King's butler (short, three-fingered, feline-featured), serves the party in guest chambers — his family served the Iranians before the "Glorious Revolution." The throne room features a giant rose stained-glass window (pink/purple), a gold-clawed throne, and four statue-guarded approaches.

The session includes hero question backstory: Drizella carries her father's silver pocket watch (stolen from her mother); Mordred has a dual-sided clasp (raven/dragon heads) from his mother's 10th birthday gift that cuts him when he tries to remove it; Bigby keeps a sheep knuckle bone from childhood street games.

## Key Events

- Basilisk defeated in sewer lair; Drizella nearly dies from bite

- Basilisk eye collected as bounty proof (500 silver reward)
- Kristoff Marlo revealed as the one who raised the Basilisk for research

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Lamere** — Beast King's butler. Short, three-fingered, feline-featured with metal cuffs. Family served the Iranians before the "Glorious Revolution." Conflicted loyalties.
- **Atasha** — Female advisor to the Beast King. Primary liaison to the party. Emphasises public reward ceremony.

## Notable Quotes

- **Kristoff** (on basilisk blood): "The blood has restorative properties, it can reinvigorate a petrified person, perhaps it could be used to instill powers of magic into the mundane."
- **Mordred** (to Lamere): "I never liked the job my mom gave me either, it was very difficult to live up to her standard."

## Hero Question

"What piece of jewellery or small token does your character wear?" — Drizella: father's silver pocket watch; Mordred: raven/dragon clasp from mother; Bigby: sheep knuckle bone from childhood.

## Lore Reveals

- **Basilisk Blood Fermentation** — Can reverse petrification when aged 1-3 months in bottles. Older = more potent. Kristoff has multiple bottles at varying stages.
- **Salasarglumm Castle** — Rose stained-glass window (pink/purple), gold-clawed throne, four statue-guarded approaches, marble floors. Newly polished/rebuilt under Beast King.
- **Kristoff's Background** — Trained at Arcanian Institute, lectured at University, traveled to "Bricks" (western continent), hiding in sewers since the coup.

# Session 61 Summary

**Date:** ~Feb 2026 (Arc 8)
**YouTube:** [Session 61](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LOmCxS0lG-w)
**Arc:** Arc 8 — Everdrift / Oz / Cindershire

## Synopsis

Party still in Oz. Briar negotiates with Forbidden Forest apple trees — learns their apples were stolen by creatures from the Emerald Sea and Emerald City. Party reaches the **Emerald City** (emerald crystal construction) and discovers the "Wizard" is actually **Barnabas Ragdoll (Barnes)**, a man who arrived years ago and became multiple personas (wizard, captain, gatekeeper) using a **time-stop machine**. The machine produces immortality liquid but is broken. Barnes was tasked by King Louie before the previous wizard died. He confirms the **Ruby Slippers** are needed to save Grimordia.

## Key Events

- Party reaches Emerald City in Oz
- Wizard of Oz revealed as Barnabas Ragdoll — single man using broken time-stop machine for multiple personas
- Machine produces immortality liquid but is broken
- Ruby Slippers confirmed as quest item needed to save Grimordia

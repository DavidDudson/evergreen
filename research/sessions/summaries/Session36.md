# Session 36 Summary

**Date:** ~May 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 36](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3dZAh5Kjf_o)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

**MAJOR LORE SESSION.** Lancelot reveals Mordred's true parentage: he is the **biological son of King Arthur** and an unnamed queen (red-haired, beautiful, kind). The queen died in childbirth; Arthur let her die unburied and refused to save her when Merlin suggested he could. **Morgana** stole baby Mordred immediately after the queen's death and vanished into the Darkwood. Lancelot was present at the birth and attempted to retrieve the baby — he fought a shapeshifting beast (Jabberwock-like), lost his companion Passibal, and returned alone. He buried the queen with his own armour and sword.

Lancelot recognises Mordred's sword immediately: **"You hold a third of Excalibur."** Arthur broke Excalibur into pieces after the civil war. The Stewards inherited crown pieces, not sword pieces. If a legitimate heir comes to power, the Stewards would lose the authority they gained after the civil war — within 20-25 years they'd cease to exist.

Meanwhile, Drizella researches at a tavern, learning the Camelot-Avalon war has lasted ~80 years with both sides draining each other. Neither are "good guys" but Camelot is "slightly more moral" now.

The **Scrying Orb** is revealed as sentient — it calls itself "Looking Glass Orb of Scrying" with three rules: all questions must be rhymed, cannot find anyone without a name/face, and cannot find people with divination beyond its DC. It activates when all party members touch it simultaneously.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Lancelot** (full reveal) — Former Knight of Camelot. Present at Mordred's birth. Buried Arthur's queen. Deeply hostile to Merlin's legacy. Came to Pebblecreek seeking a changeling of the right age to be Arthur's heir.
- **Passibal** — Lancelot's companion who died during the baby retrieval mission. Killed by shapeshifting beast.

## Lore Reveals

- **Mordred's Parentage** — Son of King Arthur + unnamed queen. Queen: red-haired, beautiful, kind. Died in childbirth. Arthur refused to bury her.
- **Morgana Stole Mordred** — Immediately after the queen's death. Vanished into Darkwood. Raised him as her own.
- **Caliburn = One-Third Excalibur** — Arthur broke Excalibur after the civil war. Three pieces: sword (Caliburn + 2 others), crown (split among Stewards).
- **Merlin's Binding** — Absolute magical servitude to Pendragon bloodline since before Uther. Cannot be free while any Pendragon lives.
- **Scrying Orb Rules** — Sentient. (1) Questions must rhyme. (2) Needs name/face to find anyone. (3) Cannot exceed its divination DC.
- **Civil War** — Caused by Arthur's grief after queen's death. 10+ years, thousands dead. Rival Hival claimed succession.

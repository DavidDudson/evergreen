## Synopsis

Party investigates Cindershire. At **Miss Muffet's orphanage**, toy dolls connected by invisible string lead to a large spider. **Octavia Muffet** (blonde, jewelled) operates an adoption/merchant service. An old woman tells the party legends of "Mythmakers Union" heroes who went through a magical mirror doorway. Children mention Muffet has a mirror. Party explores Cindershire's fortress-like architecture, noting recent safety redesigns and the Archduke's absence.

# Session 34 Summary

**Date:** ~Apr 2025 (Arc 6)
**YouTube:** [Session 34](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IQC5yeIDnk)
**Arc:** Arc 6 — Pebblecreek

## Synopsis

The party travels via fey trail from the sunken temple in Goback to **Pebblecreek**, an Underdark city beneath the Rings. Along the way they encounter a group of **Red Cap Fey** (10 small knee-high humanoids) cursed by "the great demon Merlin" to kill 10,000 foes before leaving the woods. They've killed 9,998 and desperately need 2 more. The party refuses to help them murder.

At the **Order of the Eleventh Eye** headquarters (beneath Reeds/Ersatz University), the party meets **Amelia** — a cursed-to-never-sleep member researching cleric spell evolution. Major lore drops about Merlin:

**The Mirror of Aretas** (called Mirror of Veritas here) gave a cruel Empress power. Merlin and **Craraven** found it, tricked the Empress, and trapped her inside the mirror — then scattered the shards across the world. This is **Bloody Mary's** origin. Merlin swore a pact binding himself to a high-ranking Empire member and all descendants — the **Pendragon bloodline**. He will only be freed when the last Pendragon dies.

The Order was forged by Merlin to democratise magic ("out of hands of kings into hands of common folk") but backfired into gatekeeping. Originally 11 leaders/artifacts; now only 4 members remain. Merlin disappeared the night of the Strife of Camlin — arrived at Reeds University the next day, stayed one night, then vanished. Something is hidden in the university.

Mother Gothel indicated the party will receive "four very special things left behind by an old friend" at Pebblecreek.

## Characters Introduced/Appearing

- **Amelia** — Order of the Eleventh Eye member. Cursed to never sleep. Researching cleric spell evolution. Found on island off Avalon coast.
- **Red Cap Fey** (x10) — Knee-high fey cursed by Merlin. Must kill 10,000 before leaving the woods (at 9,998). Desperate to escape. Curse breaks only via death of a creature that "hates" them.

## Lore Reveals

- **Bloody Mary's Origin** — Was a cruel Empress. Merlin and Craraven trapped her in the Mirror of Aretas, scattered shards worldwide.
- **Merlin's Oath** — Bound to a high-ranking Empire member (Pendragon ancestor). Pact binds him and all his "children." Freed only when last Pendragon dies.
- **Craraven** — Merlin's ally who helped overthrow the Arrnon Empire. Eventually diverged from Merlin. Fate unknown.
- **Order of the Eleventh Eye** — Founded by Merlin to democratise magic. Originally 11 leaders/artifacts. Now only 4 members remain. 10 of 11 artifacts once held.
- **Fey Trail** — Magical passage from sunken temple in Goback. Demi-plane connecting to Port Newer, Margate, Avalon, and Pebblecreek.
- **Red Cap Curse** — Merlin cursed them to kill 10,000. Breaks only when a creature that hates them dies.

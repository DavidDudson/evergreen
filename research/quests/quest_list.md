# Quest List — EvergreenNova

Active quest threads as of Session 66 (06-03-2026).

---

## Active Quests

### 1. The Sunderance
**Status:** Critical / ongoing
**Summary:** A periodic catastrophic event that reshapes the world. Savvan predicted it would occur within 12–18 months of Session 12 — meaning it is now imminent. At the Sunderance, Arcadia (a sunken fey isle) is expected to rise, and the Horned King's tomb beneath Rymes is expected to open. The party must find Arcadia before it does.
**Key NPCs:** Savvan, Dean Marikosa, the druids (decimated), the Horned King
**Locations:** Arcadia (will rise at "Flotsam"), Rymes (Horned King's tomb is below the city)

---

### 2. Drizella's Heart
**Status:** Critical / urgent
**Summary:** Mirror shards and a living antiseed are growing inside Drizella's heart. The antiseed roots have spoken: *"I will leave my roots deeper still."* If the mirror and antiseed meet fully, it could trigger the Horned King's return. Queen Maeve offered to surgically remove it — in exchange for delivering Drizella to Aspenthorn. Briar has agreed to this bargain.
**Key NPCs:** Drizella, Queen Maeve, the Horned King (threat)
**Flags:** `drizella_heart_antiseed`, `drizella_mirror_eye`, `briar_maeve_bargain`

---

### 3. The Glass Slippers / Wish Ring
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** The Wish Ring was used on 30 April 348 to destroy the druids beneath Emberhall. To undo the spell, the Ruby Slippers (now split into the Glass Slippers of Cindershire) must be restored. One slipper was retrieved from Cinderella's court after combat with Captain Sharmaine. The second is still missing.
**Key NPCs:** Cinderella, Carabosse (the Fairy Godmother), Wizard of Oz (Barnabas Ragdoll)
**Locations:** Cindershire, Emberhall (under search)
**Flags:** `glass_slipper_one_retrieved`

---

### 4. Finding Arcadia
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Arcadia will rise *"where the early tree blossoms"* in a place currently named **Flotsam**. The druids (decimated by the Wish Ring) hold an ally who can lead the party there. The party needs the restored Ruby Slippers first.
**Key NPCs:** Druids, Polko (gave Flotsam clue), Dean Marikosa
**Locations:** Flotsam (unknown coordinates), Emberhall

---

### 5. Nimue's Redemption
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Nimue (the Lady of the Lake) is under Morgana Le Fay's magical compulsion. She briefly broke free to give Mordred Caliburn, saying: *"The waters of Pendragon can free me."* Her soul is described as unclean. Until freed, she remains a tool of the Silverheart Court.
**Key NPCs:** Nimue, Mordred, the waters of Pendragon
**Locations:** Pendragon (Camelot's seat of power)

---

### 6. Medraut
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Mordred's mirror-image — a construct made in his likeness by Queen Maeve, but lacking his kindness. Shares Mordred's face, bears scars. His words to Mordred: *"What she did to **us**."* Morgana warned Mordred that Medraut is important and *"arranged the entire continent"* to give him answers. Their relationship is unresolved.
**Key NPCs:** Medraut, Mordred, Morgana Le Fay, Queen Maeve

---

### 7. Robert Tremaine
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Drizella's father, held captive in a room of mirrors by Queen Maeve. Freed (briefly) at Everdrift. He is an archer and told Drizella to *"escape through mirrors."* His current location is unknown.
**Key NPCs:** Robert Tremaine, Drizella, Queen Maeve

---

### 8. Anastasia Tremaine
**Status:** Active / recently resolved
**Summary:** Drizella's sister. Placed under a *modify memory* spell by Queen Maeve (memories stored in an object). Her eyes cleared when Captain Sharmaine died in Session 66 — her memories were held in him. What she now knows and what she'll do is unresolved.
**Key NPCs:** Anastasia Tremaine, Drizella, Cinderella, Sharmaine
**Flags:** `anastasia_memories_restored`

---

### 9. Morgana's Three Selves
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Morgana Le Fay killed the Three-Faced Lady in Oz and split herself into three: **Morgana** (memories/heart), **Queen Maeve** (magic/ambition), **Lady Tremaine** ("other things"). Each is diminished. Lady Tremaine appeared dead (confirmed by Greta) but is apparently still alive (Polko confirmed in Session 66). The three fragments appearing together at Everdrift caused chaos. Reconnecting or confronting them is a major thread.
**Key NPCs:** Morgana Le Fay, Queen Maeve, Lady Tremaine, the Three-Faced Lady (dead)

---

### 10. The Cailleach
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** The *"true mother of hags"* — an ancient, primordial being. Briar encountered her while dreamwalking in Morso's orphanage. A figure in the children's dreams was collecting their screams and fears; this figure panicked at the Cailleach's name and at *"Maleficent."* Connection to Maleficent and Grandmother Malyr unclear.
**Key NPCs:** The Cailleach, Maleficent, Grandmother Malyr, Miss Muffet
**Locations:** Morso (orphanage dreamscape)

---

### 11. The Blight / Not-Deer
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** A plague spreading from the Darkwoods without contact. Victims dream of drowning, chant *"As Above, So Below,"* and die within five days. Deer-like creatures called Not-Deer are appearing — physical manifestations of the Horned King, speaking his words. The High Matron Mayana is investigating. Possible leads: a cyclops, undead at Hemlock Downs, smugglers' den.
**Key NPCs:** High Matron Mayana, the Horned King (manifesting), Not-Deer
**Locations:** Cindershire, Hemlock Downs

---

### 12. Briar's Dragon Lairs Curse
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Briar Rose died and made a deal with **the Fool** to return to life. The price: something *"truly random"* occurring (she vomited a blacksmith's tongs). The curse will only fully break once she sleeps in the lairs of **five dragons**. Progress: **0 of 5**.
**Key NPCs:** The Fool, Briar Rose
**Progress:** 0 / 5 dragon lairs visited

---

### 13. Prince Phillip
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Briar Rose's twin soul — whatever happens to one happens to the other. Augusta (Mab's sister) suggested Phillip may have *"experienced the same thing Briar did"* — meaning he may also have been sleeping somewhere for 300 years and may now be awake. He may be findable.
**Key NPCs:** Prince Phillip, Briar Rose, Augusta
**Flags:** `briar_awake`

---

### 14. Bigby's Brother (The Big Bad Wolf)
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Bigby's older brother is the legendary Big Bad Wolf. He attempted to assassinate Bigby on behalf of the Hogson Brothers. Their rivalry is ongoing. Flycatcher cannot remember the brother's name. Bigby saw his brother's image in Mary's mirror boon.
**Key NPCs:** Bigby, the Big Bad Wolf, the Hogson Brothers
**Flags:** `bigby_brother_rival`

---

### 15. Oberon's Murder Investigation
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** King Oberon was murdered on **30 April 348**. The assassin used a blade identical to Bigby's exact dagger. Ten minutes of memory are missing from the minds of many fey who were present. Tinker Bell confirmed the wish spell was also cast on that exact date beneath Emberhall. Two crimes on the same day — are they connected?
**Key NPCs:** Oberon, Bigby, Tinker Bell, Grandma Malyr (possible suspect)
**Locations:** Emberhall, Everdrift

---

### 16. Grandmother Malyr
**Status:** Active / suspicious
**Summary:** Malyr has promised Miss Muffet a safe glade for her children after the Sunderance — implying she has unusual knowledge of or control over what comes after. Mordred read Grandmother Alpine's fate as *"traitor."* The Cailleach is also connected to Malyr (Muffet mentioned her name). Her true allegiance is unclear.
**Key NPCs:** Grandmother Malyr, Miss Muffet, Grandmother Alpine, the Cailleach

---

### 17. Robson Wellgood in Mercia
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Robson Wellgood was a prisoner of Queen Maeve at Everdrift. He deliberately threw a card game against Bigby and told him: *"Find me in Mercia."* He was listed as a Silverheart Court member in older intelligence but appears to be working against Maeve. He may be a double agent or recently turned ally.
**Key NPCs:** Robson Wellgood, Bigby
**Locations:** Mercia

---

### 18. Merlin's Imprisonment
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** Merlin (founder of the Order of the Eleventh Eye and Briar's distant patron) has been imprisoned by Queen Maeve. Briar negotiated his release — in exchange for allowing Maeve to operate on Drizella at Aspenthorn. Maeve warned: *"You won't want him back when you know why he was imprisoned."*
**Key NPCs:** Merlin, Queen Maeve, Briar
**Flags:** `briar_maeve_bargain`

---

### 19. The Mysterious "M"
**Status:** Active
**Summary:** An unknown figure who controls police and city officials in Xyander, owns docks and boats across Arrnonland, and put **Eamus Hogson** in charge of antiseed distribution. Flycatcher confirmed at least five antiseeds were distributed. Captain Irene Adler was mentioned in connection with Pebblecreek investigations. "M" may be a person, title, or conspiracy.
**Key NPCs:** Eamus Hogson, Flycatcher, Captain Irene Adler
**Locations:** Xyander, Arrnonland ports

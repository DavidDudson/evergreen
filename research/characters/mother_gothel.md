# Dame Gothel (Mother Gothel)

**Title:** Former All-Mother of the Cruthic (Thrice-Triple) Covens
**Status:** Unknown — believed dead ~50 years before the campaign (year ~311)
**Age:** 100+
**Alignment:** Neutral (complex)

---

## Appearance

Pointed ears, a hooked nose, curly grey hair, weathered skin. Wears many layers of shawls and smells faintly of woodsmoke and herbs. Despite her age, her eyes are unnervingly sharp.

---

## Role in the World

Dame Gothel was the most powerful All-Mother the Thrice-Triple Covens had ever seen. Her death (or disappearance) in ~311 triggered the **All-Mother Succession Crisis**, a period of infighting among the covens that weakened them considerably.

She previously possessed **Caliburn** — keeping it safe for the Pendragon bloodline before Mordred came into it. She worked with Nimue (before Nimue turned) to keep the sword hidden.

She warned the party about Caliburn's true nature: that it carves truth in the world, not just in flesh, and that wielding it draws the attention of things older than the Sunderance.

---

## The Cruthic Covens

The Covens she led worship the Three-Faced Lady. Three hundred and thirty-three covens, spread across Arrnonland and beyond. Their ranks:

- **Sister** — initiate
- **Mother** — full witch
- **Auntie** — senior witch, keeper of tradition
- **Grandmother** — head of a coven
- **All-Mother** — head of all covens; chosen by the Grandmothers

Colours: black, scarlet, purple.

---

## Relationship to Other Characters

- **Morgana Le Fay** — Dame Gothel trained Morgana in the Old Ways long before Morgana became the avatar of the Three-Faced Lady. Their relationship ended in betrayal.
- **Nimue** — collaborated until Nimue's fall to the Silverheart Court
- **Mordred** — warned him directly about Caliburn; may have been aware of his Pendragon blood before he was

---

## Dialogue Notes

Voice: slow, deliberate, uses old forms of speech. Never explains directly — always speaks in the third truth ("what you're really asking is..."). Knows more than she says and says more than she seems to.

Secrets she guards:
- What really happened to the Three-Faced Lady
- What Dame Gothel traded to live this long
- Whether she is truly dead or merely hiding

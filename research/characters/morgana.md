# Morgana Le Fay

**Also known as:** The Begot Queen, Morgana Pendragon, The Wicked Witch
**Status:** Alive (approximately 70–80 years old; appears ~45)
**Born:** ~275
**Alignment:** Chaotic / complex antagonist

---

## Appearance

Pale skin. Black hair heavily streaked with white. Pink eyes — a trait she shares suspiciously with Queen Maeve. Strong resemblance to Queen Maeve, Morgause, and Lady Tremaine (the relationships are not yet explained). Travels with a crow familiar. Her crest is two crows beside a chalice.

---

## History

Sister of **King Arthur Pendragon**. Arthur attempted to use Morgana to produce an heir through a blood ritual, causing the **Strife of Camlin** — a major historical rupture between them.

Morgana became the **avatar of the Three-Faced Lady** — the goddess's mortal vessel. She then used Caliburn to cut the goddess out of herself, reportedly murdering the deity ~80 years ago. The consequences of this act are still rippling through the world.

She was first killed in 311 in Oz. She is currently alive, dwelling deep in the **Broceliande** (the Darkwoods). Her longevity is tied to the magic she absorbed as the goddess's avatar.

She is **Mordred's adoptive mother**. Mordred fled her care when he came to understand the weight of what she was preparing him for.

---

## Abilities

- Controls crow swarms
- Enters and controls dreams
- Teleportation
- Negative energy manipulation
- Illusion mastery
- Soul seizure (can temporarily trap a soul)
- Telepathy
- 9th-level spellcasting

---

## Relationship to the Three-Faced Lady

When Morgana was the goddess's avatar, she had access to the complete portfolio of the Three-Faced Lady: occult magic, time, balance, and love. When she severed that connection (if she truly did), she kept much of the power but lost the goddess's guidance. The faith of the Thrice-Triple Covens has been fractured ever since.

---

## Relationship to Other Characters

- **Arthur Pendragon** — estranged siblings; Arthur tried to use her for dynastic ends
- **Mordred** — adoptive son; she watches him from the Broceliande
- **Guinevere** (†) — lover; the Strife of Camlin involved all three

- **Queen Maeve** — share pink eyes; unknown relationship; Maeve is suspected by some to be a version of Morgana

- **Memphis Cadwallader** — knows each other; mutual wariness

---

## Dialogue Notes

Voice: measured, precise, and ancient. Every sentence is chosen. She does not raise her voice — she never needs to. Her patience is geological.

She does not consider herself a villain. In her view, she severed herself from a goddess to become free, and she is paying the price with everything she has. What she wants from Briar Rose — if she wants anything — she will not say directly.

Secrets:
- Whether the Three-Faced Lady is truly dead or merely sleeping inside Morgana
- Her plans for Mordred
- What she knows about the Seventh Sunderance that she hasn't told anyone

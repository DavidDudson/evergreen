Some characters sit on the border of factions. Their tag reflects where they *currently* draw power, not where they were born.

| Name | Tagged | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Drizella Tremaine** | darkwoods | Antiseed, nightmare domain, Faceless Soldier. Actively moving toward something else. |
| **Morgana Le Fay** | darkwoods | Lives in the Broceliande. Severed a goddess. Not simply evil, but methods are Darkwoods. |
| **Mordred** | greenwoods | Despite Pendragon (Cities) blood. Wandering oracle path and Caliburn connection. Contested. |
| **Queen Maeve** | darkwoods | Cities-born ruler, but Silverheart Court goals are Darkwoods. |
| **Nimue** | darkwoods | Under compulsion. True self is greenwoods (former Three-Faced Lady avatar). "Waters of Pendragon can free me." |
| **Robson Wellgood** | darkwoods | Silverheart Court member but appears to be defecting. Told Bigby: "Find me in Mercia." |

# Player Characters — EvergeenNova Wiki

These are the members (past and present) of the **Mythmakers Union** adventuring party.
In the Evergreen game, Briar Rose is the playable character; the others appear as NPCs.

---

## Briar Rose (Aurora Plantagenet)
**Class:** Druid
**Status:** Alive — the playable character
**Born:** 287, Grimordia
**Also known as:** Aurora Plantagenet, Princess Aurora

Briar Rose was cursed by Maleficent on her 16th birthday. She was put into a centuries-long sleep by a deal brokered by Memphis Cadwallader (~300 years ago), waking in the present having missed everything. Member of the Mythmakers Union; bearer of the briar — a magical ability to heal what others have broken. Her twin soul is Prince Phillip (whatever happens to one happens to the other).

Parents: Marquis Stefan and Marquise Leah of Grimordia (found petrified in the palace, S51). Her family guards a **wish ring** — Maleficent cursed her because she demanded it. Death prophecy (S56): dies *in the arms of her beloved*. Made the Fool's deal (S65-66): died, returned to life, must sleep in 5 dragon lairs. Her familiar Bud was killed by Henry Borsch (S44). The Phoenix Egg that hatched in Oz was named **Darian** in Darian Sand's memory.

## Mordred
**Class:** Oracle (Battle)
**Status:** Alive
**Born:** 336
**Race:** Changeling
**From:** Pendragon (Camelot)

Adoptive son of Morgana Le Fay; wields Caliburn. Yellow/gold eyes, black hair with a white streak, growing feathers — the marks of a changeling. Born to the Pendragon bloodline through a spell on Arthur and Guinevere. Haunted by "The Stranger." Fled from Morgana's shadow in the Broceliande. Wields Caliburn — the truth-carving sword that also destroys Antiseeds.

**Personality:** Cautious, introspective, carries the weight of prophecy. Speaks in carefully considered words.

**NPC role:** Wandering oracle; warns about the Sunderance and the Silverheart Court. Can reveal lore about Caliburn, the Pendragon line, and Morgana.

**True parentage** (S36): biological son of King Arthur and an unnamed red-haired queen who died in childbirth. Morgana stole him as a baby. Lancelot was present at his birth. Caliburn is one-third of the original Excalibur (confirmed by Lancelot, S36). Also wields Clarent/Caledfwlch (used to free Bloody Mary from the Mirror of Aretas, S57). Lost his left hand to Baron Drazag (S27); replaced by a cursed 9th-rank magical black gauntlet from Morgana. Died protecting Bigby from Nimue's Harm spell (S32); resurrected by Mother Gothel. Death prophecy (S56): dies *very soon, at the hands of his father*. Left the party temporarily to rescue Galahad (S46) after Morgana said he was 'suffering.' An infernal entity holds a binding contract over him, claiming him during death: 'We've got a deal' (S32).

## Drizella Tremaine
**Class:** Cleric (Nightmare Domain)
**Status:** Alive
**Race:** Fleshwarp (human, heavily altered)
**From:** Quillwilde

Follower of The Faceless Soldier. Her skin is translucent — internal organs are faintly visible. She has an Antiseed in her heart, and the Mythmakers are working to remove it. Her sister is Anastasia Tremaine. She suspects Memphis Cadwallader was involved in the Glass Slipper curse that affected the Tremaine family.

**Personality:** Unsettling, deeply devout, surprisingly compassionate. Her appearance frightens most people before she speaks.

**NPC role:** Temple figure; can offer information about the Faceless Soldier, the Antiseeds, and the nature of nightmares. Rare item exchanges.

**Engineered from birth** by Carabosse, Bloody Mary, and Lady Tremaine to free Mary from the mirror (confirmed S55). Made first direct contact with Bloody Mary (S14): ate a mirror shard, gaining Locate Object at the cost of 10 max HP and permanently losing the ability to feel joy. Replaced her eyeball with a mirror shard (S47), giving Mary permanent scrying access. Goblins later stole the mirror-shard eye for the Goblin King. Caliburn chose her as keeper (S33). Stole Beast King's diary (Codex of Mystic Lore, S32). Death prophecy (S56): dies *content at her desk*. Provisional Medicaster licence from Matron Simone (S3).

## Bigby (Joseph Bigby)
**Class:** Investigator / Rogue
**Status:** Alive
**Race:** Beastkin (lupine)
**From:** Xyander

A wolf-beastkin; former crewman under Captain Hook. Connected to the legend of the Big Bad Wolf. Gruff, investigative, originally came to Arrnonland following a trail of strange disappearances.

**Personality:** Gruff, world-weary, dry-humoured. Speaks plainly. Nose rarely wrong.

**NPC role:** Information broker; can share rumours, investigate leads, and provide background on Xyander and the Redeye pandemic.

**Past** references assassination work in Xander (Irene Adler: 'Excellent work on Xander. A shame you were too slow,' S38). Blackmailed by Flycatcher (Edward Basque, Hogson supervisor) to break into Sunspire jail (S37). Won architecture master class at Ersatz University; offered future teaching position by Dean Marikosa (S45). Death prophecy (S56): dies *at the hands of a bitter rival*.

## Former Members

### Merida
Archer, from the highlands. Her mother is 'the Rot Queen' — powerful enough that a hag fled at the name (S6). Speaks the Darkwood/hag tongue. Her father Fergus lost his leg fighting the Great Bear in a northern rite of passage (S17). Began the three witch initiation trials under Grandmother Hazel (S13). Left the party.

### Sibylla
Seer. Was once a powerful 16th-17th level witch (~90 years old). Accidentally de-aged to a powerless 17-year-old while trying to cure Red Eye disease (S15 flashback). Previously a child-eating hag; on a redemption arc. Exiled from the Covens for forbidden magic but covered under the Haggish Concordant (law 15). Left the party.

### Nik Jones
Magus. Mortal origins with infernal blood — his father is a demon currently on this plane (confirmed by Dean Marikosa, S8). Every use of magic signals the father. His soul was used as collateral with Davy Jones by Captain Hook. Fire magic and shadow-teleportation. Left the party.

### Darian Sand (†)
**Class:** Sorcerer
**Status:** Deceased
**Race:** Human
**From:** Streets; mentored by Merlin

Sorcerer; human; raised on the streets; mentored by Merlin. Joined the party via teleportation accident in Oronia (S14). Had a parakeet familiar called Nightshade. Died at the Pebblecreek docks fighting the Silverheart Court (S41). His body was stolen by Nimue after a naval battle (S43). The Phoenix that hatched in Oz was named Darian in his honour.

# Player Characters — EvergeenNova Wiki

These are the members (past and present) of the **Mythmakers Union** adventuring party.
In the Evergreen game, Briar Rose is the playable character; the others appear as NPCs.

---

## Briar Rose (Aurora Plantagenet)
**Class:** Druid
**Status:** Alive — the playable character
**Born:** 287, Grimordia
**Also known as:** Aurora Plantagenet, Princess Aurora

Briar Rose was cursed by Maleficent on her 16th birthday. She was put into a centuries-long sleep by a deal brokered by Memphis Cadwallader (~300 years ago), waking in the present having missed everything. Member of the Mythmakers Union; bearer of the briar — a magical ability to heal what others have broken. Her twin soul is Prince Phillip (whatever happens to one happens to the other).

---

## Mordred
**Class:** Oracle (Battle)
**Status:** Alive
**Born:** 336
**Race:** Changeling
**From:** Pendragon (Camelot)

Adoptive son of Morgana Le Fay; wields Caliburn. Yellow/gold eyes, black hair with a white streak, growing feathers — the marks of a changeling. Born to the Pendragon bloodline through a spell on Arthur and Guinevere. Haunted by "The Stranger." Fled from Morgana's shadow in the Broceliande. Wields Caliburn — the truth-carving sword that also destroys Antiseeds.

**Personality:** Cautious, introspective, carries the weight of prophecy. Speaks in carefully considered words.

**NPC role:** Wandering oracle; warns about the Sunderance and the Silverheart Court. Can reveal lore about Caliburn, the Pendragon line, and Morgana.

---

## Drizella Tremaine
**Class:** Cleric (Nightmare Domain)
**Status:** Alive
**Race:** Fleshwarp (human, heavily altered)
**From:** Quillwilde

Follower of The Faceless Soldier. Her skin is translucent — internal organs are faintly visible. She has an Antiseed in her heart, and the Mythmakers are working to remove it. Her sister is Anastasia Tremaine. She suspects Memphis Cadwallader was involved in the Glass Slipper curse that affected the Tremaine family.

**Personality:** Unsettling, deeply devout, surprisingly compassionate. Her appearance frightens most people before she speaks.

**NPC role:** Temple figure; can offer information about the Faceless Soldier, the Antiseeds, and the nature of nightmares. Rare item exchanges.

---

## Bigby (Joseph Bigby)
**Class:** Investigator / Rogue
**Status:** Alive
**Race:** Beastkin (lupine)
**From:** Xyander

A wolf-beastkin; former crewman under Captain Hook. Connected to the legend of the Big Bad Wolf. Gruff, investigative, originally came to Arrnonland following a trail of strange disappearances.

**Personality:** Gruff, world-weary, dry-humoured. Speaks plainly. Nose rarely wrong.

**NPC role:** Information broker; can share rumours, investigate leads, and provide background on Xyander and the Redeye pandemic.

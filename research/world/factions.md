# Factions of EvergeenNova

---

## Thrice-Triple Covens (Cruthic Covens)

The dominant witch order of Arrnonland. 333 covens spread across the known world.

**Patron deity:** The Three-Faced Lady (currently in unknown state after Morgana severed the avatar bond)
**Colours:** Black, scarlet, purple
**Ranks:** Sister → Mother → Auntie → Grandmother → All-Mother
**Current All-Mother:** Tashanna
**Former All-Mother:** Dame Gothel (deceased/missing since ~311)

The covens have been weakened since the All-Mother Succession Crisis. Their relationship to the Three-Faced Lady is currently fractured — no new All-Mother has been able to establish the old connection to the goddess.

---

## Silverheart Court

A secret alliance engineering the return of the Horned King.

**Leader:** Queen Maeve of Avalon
**Members include:** Maleficent, the Hogson Brothers (Peter & John), the Beast King (defeated), Lady Piques, Nimue, Medraut, Robson Wellgood
**Goal:** Resurrect the Horned King by grafting Antiseeds to wicked hearts, eventually producing a host that transforms fully

---

## Order of the Eleventh Eye (OXIE)

Ancient mage order founded by Merlin. Keepers of the Staves of Power (10+ staves created from the eyes of a slain beast).

**Founder:** Merlin
**Status:** Greatly diminished since the Fall of Arcadia (year 276); several staves scattered or stolen
**Notable current holders:**
- Staff of Action — Emilia Zeneba
- Staff of Insight — Osiris Krex

---

## Red Dawn / Red Hand

Militant religious order. Worship the Burning God. Based in Radishar.

**Mission:** Purge witches and Fey from the world
**Ranks:** Emberlings → Torch Bearers → Firekeepers → Bonfires
**Relationship to the party:** Hostile — Drizella is a witch-follower and the party includes a druid

---

## Fey Courts

After the falling-out between Titania and Maeve, the old Seelie/Unseelie division fractured into four seasonal courts:

| Court | Leader | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Spring | Lady Caelia | Birth, renewal |
| Summer | King Louie | Rules Everdrift; hedonistic; generally friendly |
| Autumn | Jackie Brush | Harvest, endings |
| Winter | Jack Frost | Cold, isolation; tense relations with mortals |

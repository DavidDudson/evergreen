# Faction Alignment Map

Three power factions divide the world of Evergreen. Every NPC, organisation,
and lore entry is aligned with one of them. The alignment tag appears in every
`DialogueScript`'s `keyword_tags` and in the `LoreBook` entries that record
witnessed conversations.

---

## Greenwoods — *Dame Gothel*

**Ethos:** Natural magic, memory, preservation. The Greenwoods faction believes
the forest is alive and must be tended. They oppose the corruption spreading
from the Darkwoods and distrust the transactional power of the Cities.

## Darkwoods — *Bloody Mary*

**Ethos:** Power through corruption, nightmare, and reshaping. The Darkwoods
faction believes the old world must be unmade so something greater can rise —
though what "greater" means depends on who you ask. Many members are tools of
the Silverheart Court without knowing it.

## Cities — *Memphis Cadwallader*

**Ethos:** Power through agreement, commerce, and accumulated obligation.
The Cities faction believes all things can be negotiated and all power
ultimately flows through who owes whom what. Cadwallader does not rule
the Cities — he simply knows every debt in them.

## Notes on Complex Alignments

Some characters sit on the border of two factions and their alignment reflects
where they *currently* draw their power, not necessarily where they were born
or who they once served:

- **Drizella Tremaine** — Tagged `darkwoods` (Antiseed, nightmare domain, Faceless Soldier). She is actively trying to move toward something else; this tension is central to her arc.
- **Morgana Le Fay** — Tagged `darkwoods` (lives in the Broceliande, severs a goddess from the world). She is not simply evil — but her methods and territory are Darkwoods.
- **Mordred** — Tagged `greenwoods` despite Pendragon (Cities) blood. His wandering oracle path and connection to Caliburn place him in forest alignment. His blood is contested.
- **Queen Maeve** — Cities-born ruler, but her goals are Darkwoods. She is listed under Darkwoods to reflect the Silverheart Court allegiance.

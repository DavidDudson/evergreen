# Key Lore Events — EvergeenNova

Timeline uses the campaign's in-world year numbering (Fourth Age).

---

## Fall of Arcadia (Year 276)

The Horned King — once benevolent, long since corrupted — ruled the island of Arcadia. A coalition of fey and mortals used the **Mirror of Aretas** to destroy him. The island sank. The Horned King's essence was not destroyed — it fragmented into the Antiseeds.

The Mirror of Aretas subsequently trapped the soul of **Bloody Mary**, a dangerous empress who had seized control of OXIE from Merlin.

---

## Birth of Briar Rose / Maleficent's Curse (Year 287–303)

Briar Rose (Aurora Plantagenet) was born in 287. When she turned 16 (year 303), Maleficent — archfey dragon and ruler of Pegaroth — cursed her. The nature of the curse required a deal: **Tobias Gold** (Memphis Cadwallader) brokered an arrangement that put Briar Rose to sleep for 300+ years instead of killing her. The favour owed to Cadwallader was left open.

---

## Strife of Camlin (Year ~300)

King Arthur attempted to use his sister Morgana in a blood ritual to produce a Pendragon heir. This caused the Strife of Camlin — a major political rupture between them. The ritual produced **Mordred** (born 336), a Changeling made from the blood of Arthur and Queen Guinevere through Morgana's magic.

---

## Morgana Severs the Avatar Bond (~Year 281)

Morgana Le Fay, then the avatar of the Three-Faced Lady, used Caliburn to cut the goddess out of herself. This reportedly killed or severely wounded the deity. The Thrice-Triple Covens' connection to their patron has been fractured ever since.

---

## Death of All-Mother Gothel (~Year 311)

Dame Gothel, the most powerful All-Mother the Cruthic Covens ever had, died (or disappeared) around year 311. This triggered the **All-Mother Succession Crisis** — years of infighting among the covens that left them weakened and fractured.

Morgana also first died in 311, in Oz.

---

## Queen Maeve's Coup (Year 323)

Queen Maeve seized control of Avalon from its previous ruler in 323. She has since built it into a military power now at war with Camelot for thirty years.

---

## Maleficent Discovers the Antiseeds (Year 343)

Maleficent, an archfey dragon with deep roots in old magic, discovered the Antiseeds — the fragmented essence of the Horned King — and brought this knowledge to the Silverheart Court. The plan to resurrect the Horned King began here.

---

## Beast King's Conquest (Year 346)

The Beast King, a Silverheart Court member, conquered Salasarglumm. Later defeated by the Mythmakers Union.

---

## Present Day (~Year 361)

The Seventh Sunderance is overdue and imminent. The Silverheart Court accelerates its plans. The Blight plague spreads through Arrnonland. The Redeye pandemic spreads from Xyander. Briar Rose has woken.

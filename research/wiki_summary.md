## The World

**Evergreen** is a world blanketed in never-ending magical forest. Mortal civilisation exists in clearings called **Glades**, connected by dangerous woodland paths. The forest itself is alive and the domain of the Faefolk.

### The Four Peoples

1. **Mortal Folk** — humans, dwarves, and kin; live in the Glades
2. **Faefolk (Fey)** — magical beings of the deep forest; vulnerable to cold iron
3. **Wild Folk** — non-Fey mortals living outside Glades (gnomes, beastfolk, etc.)
4. **Burrishfolk** — mountain and underground dwellers

Other peoples: Changelings (hagborn), Hags (witches-turned-Fey), Satyrs, Vampires, Grendel's Folk (half-giants), Ghorans, Gnolls

### The Sunderance

A world-reshaping magical catastrophe that occurs roughly every 300 years. Six have happened. The **Seventh Sunderance** is long overdue (~352 years since the last) and is prophesied to arrive within 12–18 months. Each sunderance reshapes the forests, unmakes borders, and destroys civilisations.

## Key Artefacts

### The Three Swords (split from original Excalibur)
- **Excalibur** — displayed in Pendragon; wieldable only by Pendragon bloodline
- **Caliburn** — wielded by Mordred; carves truth; destroys Antiseeds; also only for Pendragon blood
- **Caledfwlch** — missing

### Staves of Power
10 ancient staves created from the eyes of a slain beast; once protected by OXIE. Several now scattered or stolen.

### Antiseeds
Parasitic seeds from the Tree of Arcadia. Contain the Horned King's essence. Graft to the hearts of wicked people. Seven true seeds + four precursor seeds known. Drizella has one in her heart.

### Mirror of Aretas
The 11th "eye" of the slain beast. Used to defeat the Horned King. Traps Bloody Mary. Currently shattered/scattered.

## Current Threats

1. **The Seventh Sunderance** — imminent; no one knows exactly when or what it will cause
2. **The Silverheart Court** — engineering the return of the Horned King via Antiseeds
3. **The Blight** — lethal deer-corpse plague; victims chant "As Above, So Below" before death
4. **The Redeye** — zombie-like pandemic from Xyander/Westfold; spreads via biting
5. **Queen Maeve** — collecting Mirror of Aretas shards and artefacts
6. **Bloody Mary** — imprisoned in the Mirror but active through reflective surfaces

## The Horned King

Once a benevolent fey/god, the Horned King (also: Blighted Lord, Prince of Rot, Nightmare King, Merry Stag Lord) turned cruel and ruled the island of Arcadia. He was destroyed in 276 (Fourth Age) by a coalition using the Mirror of Aretas. Arcadia sank. The Silverheart Court now works to resurrect him using Antiseeds — the seeds, grafted to wicked hearts, may transform a host into the Horned King himself.

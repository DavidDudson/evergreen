## The World

**Evergreen** is a world blanketed in never-ending magical forest. Mortal civilisation exists in clearings called **Glades**, connected by dangerous woodland paths. The forest itself is alive and the domain of the Faefolk.

### The Four Peoples

1. **Mortal Folk** — humans, dwarves, and kin; live in the Glades
2. **Faefolk (Fey)** — magical beings of the deep forest; vulnerable to cold iron
3. **Wild Folk** — non-Fey mortals living outside Glades (gnomes, beastfolk, etc.)
4. **Burrishfolk** — mountain and underground dwellers

Other peoples: Changelings (hagborn), Hags (witches-turned-Fey), Satyrs, Vampires, Grendel's Folk (half-giants), Ghorans, Gnolls

### The Sunderance

A world-reshaping magical catastrophe that occurs roughly every 300 years. Six have happened. The **Seventh Sunderance** is long overdue (~352 years since the last) and is prophesied to arrive within 12–18 months. Each sunderance reshapes the forests, unmakes borders, and destroys civilisations.

## Key Artefacts

### The Three Swords
- **Caledfwlch** — The original Excalibur; "the discarded blade" that touched the Horned King's hands. Has anti-scrying runes. Mordred has been carrying it disguised as "Clarent" without knowing. Only he can lift it. Used as a key to unlock Bloody Mary's prison in the Mirror.
- **Caliburn** — Entrusted to Nimue; she bears only the scabbard now. Mordred holds the blade (given to him by Nimue in Session 59, briefly freed from Morgana's compulsion). A silver dragon of light erupts when he uses it. Destroys Antiseeds. Confirms Pendragon bloodline.
- **Excalibur** — Displayed in an anvil at Pendragon; wieldable only by the Pendragon bloodline.

### Staves of Power
10 ancient staves created from the eyes of a slain beast; once protected by OXIE. Several now scattered or stolen.

### Antiseeds
Parasitic seeds from the Tree of Arcadia. Contain the Horned King's essence. Graft to the hearts of wicked people. Seven true seeds + four precursor seeds known. Drizella has one in her heart.

### Mirror of Aretas
The 11th "eye" of the slain beast. Used to defeat the Horned King at the Fall of Arcadia. Trapped Bloody Mary for ~100 years. Shattered; shards scattered across Arrnonland. **Bloody Mary was freed in Session 57** when Mordred used Caledfwlch as the key to her prison. Warning: if a mirror shard and an antiseed meet in the same host (i.e. Drizella), it could trigger the Horned King's full return — the same combination that originally killed him.

### The Ruby / Glass Slippers
Originally the Ruby Slippers of Oz; Bloody Mary took them from Barnabas Ragdoll (the Wizard of Oz). Now in Cindershire as the Glass Slippers. One slipper retrieved from Cinderella's court (Session 66). Both slippers together can undo the **Wish Ring** spell that destroyed the druids on 30 April 348.

### The Wish Ring
Used in 348 by an unknown party beneath Emberhall to destroy the druids. Tied to Oberon's murder (same date). Undoing this spell requires the restored Ruby Slippers.

### The Antiseeds
Parasitic seeds from the Tree of Arcadia. Contain the Horned King's essence. Seven true seeds + four precursor seeds known. Graft to the hearts of wicked people and slowly corrupt the host. Drizella has one growing alongside mirror shards in her heart. The roots spoke: *"I will leave my roots deeper still."* Mordred destroyed one antiseed using Caliburn in Pebblecreek (Session 41), releasing the Horned King's Tear — a memory of who he once was.

## The Horned King

Once the **Merry Stag Lord** — a beloved god of harvest, plenty, and spring who gifted meat, wine, and furs in winter and received offerings in spring. Over centuries his followers demanded more than he could give. He grew monstrous; *"the flesh melted from his bones; all that remained was the Horned King."*

He is also called the **Tuatha Slaugh** — a deity cast out of the Old Religion pantheon. He ruled Arcadia before his destruction. He was slain at the Fall of Arcadia (~Year 276, Fourth Age) by a coalition of nine Grandmother Hags, Titania, Mab, the Gold Dragon of Thurran, the Jabberwocky, Merlin, and the Green Man. The Order of the Eleventh Eye placed the Mirror of Aretas beneath his tomb (which lies under the city of Rymes).

His **Antiseeds** are fragments of the Tree of Arcadia containing pieces of his essence. His **Tears** hold memories of who he once was. The **Not-Deer** appearing in Cindershire are early manifestations of his return, saying: *"The Name is still hollow. It is not yet above as it is below."*

The Silverheart Court is engineering his return by collecting mirror shards and antiseeds. The next Sunderance is expected to open his tomb.

## Confirmed Lore from Session Recaps

### The Three-Faced Lady — Confirmed Dead
Session 60: In Oz, the party found the chapel of the Three-Faced Lady with beheaded bodies (young, middle-aged, and elderly women). Footprints of Lady Tremaine, Morgana Le Fay, and Queen Maeve led away from a pool of blood. **Morgana killed the Three-Faced Lady** and used her death to split herself into three more powerful selves. The Winged Monkeys in Oz's Forbidden Forest confirmed: the Three-Faced Lady visited Morgana and then *"she left with them and disappeared into three versions of herself."*

### Morgana's Three Selves
Morgana Pendragon is now three people: **Morgana Le Fay** (memories, emotional connections), **Queen Maeve** (magic and ambition), **Lady Tremaine** ("other things"). Each is diminished — *"each less than she once was."* All three appeared together for the first time at Everdrift (Session 59). Morgana is cursed never to speak of what she did to the Three-Faced Lady.

### Bloody Mary's Full History
Born 226 of the 6th Age. Trained by Merlin from a young age. Rose to power by age 15. Took control of the Order of the Eleventh Eye from Merlin and used it to conquer the Arrnon Empire, becoming Empress. Imprisoned in the Mirror of Aretas at age 22 (~248) by Merlin and Craven. The Empire shattered when the Mirror broke. Claims she only wants to reunite the mirror shards and antiseeds as a weapon — but this combination is the most dangerous thing in the world.

### Merlin's Last Words
Queen Titania reported that on the night Merlin disappeared, she received a message in his voice: *"Forgive me, Sister."* Tuatha De Danann avatars cannot have siblings — yet this implies Merlin had one. The Sacred Sister deity is possibly connected.

### The Parallax
A convergence of the Winter Solstice and Full Moon that amplifies all occult magic. It occurred early in the campaign (Sessions 1–2) and set many events in motion.

### Arcadia's Return
Polko confirmed in Session 66: Arcadia will rise *"where the early tree blossoms"* in a place currently named **Flotsam**. The druids (destroyed 30 April 348 by the Wish Ring) hold an ally who can guide the party there.
